Cosmetic Surgery Eyelids In Albuquerque, New Mexico

Blepharoplasty (from Greek blepharon – eyelid) in Albuquerque is a surgical correction of the eyelids, the purpose of which is to remove the overhanging skin folds of the eyelids and the “bags under the eyes.”

Plastic surgery on the upper and lower eyelids is corrected eye contour by removing excess skin or fat formations.

Today, blepharoplasty is one of the most common operations in the face.

That is why there are several different ways to correct eyelids, allowing to change the shape of the eyes, to remove various defects eyelids, including their age-related changes.

The most known surgeries are: upper eyelids surgery, lower eyelid surgery, transconjunctival lower eyelid surgery and combined (circular) surgery of the upper and lower eyelids.

Usually, blepharoplasty in Albuquerque is performed for people (usually women) aged 35 years and older, but often this operation is performed at a younger age if necessary.

Indications for blepharoplasty (eyelid surgery):

– excess skin of the upper and lower eyelids (the overhang);
– protrusion of “fatty tissue” in the upper and lower eyelids (“fatty bags of eyelids”);
– wrinkles in the lower eyelids;
– omitted corners of the eyes;
– deep wrinkles in the lower eyelid area and the eye corners;
– desire to change the shape of the eye.

Contraindications for blepharoplasty:

– dry eyes

– diseases of the cardiovascular system, and others serious diseases of internal organs
– diabetes

Blepharoplasty involves quite a serious intervention in the area of orbital fat of the eyes, so patient must be examined by an ophthalmologist before the surgery eyelid.

Moreover, if you wear glasses or contact lenses, you must show them at the consultation. Also, after consultation it is necessary attend a medical examination.

Albuquerque blepharoplasty is performed only in a hospital under general anesthesia combined with local infiltration anesthesia. The cosmetic eye surgery usually lasts from 1, 5 to 3 hours. The eyeball is not affected during the operation and this operation is absolutely safe to eyesight.

Blepharoplasty of upper eyelids

The incision on the upper eyelid is make in natural folds of the eye so that the postoperative scar did not allocated with eyes open.

Sometimes additional manipulation can also be performed on the reposition the lacrimal gland and “lift” the circular muscles of the eye, is removed adipose hernia if necessary. Excess skin of the upper eyelid are removed by excision of spindle-shaped skin flap.

Blepharoplasty of lower eyelids

Thinnest incisions are immediately below the eyelash line on the lower eyelid.

Excess Eyelid Skin Surgery In New Mexico

Through these incisions the surgeon separates the skin from the adipose tissue and muscle, removes excess fat, and often cuts the weakened skin and muscle. After that the incisions are stitched with a cosmetic seams.

In cases where the patient has excess fat in the lower eyelids and no excess skin, a method of transconjunctival blepharoplasty can be used. This method is suitable for young patients with a thicker and more elastic skin.

In this method, a tiny incision or puncture is made inside the lower eyelid (through the conjunctiva), then,the surgeon removes the excess fat. Transconjunctival blepharoplasty removes visible seams or scars on the skin and cardinally reduces the recovery period.

At the same, a facial expression is not disturbed and the shape of the eye does not change.

In recent years, more and more leading of plastic surgeons try not to remove “fatty” of a hernia in the lower eyelids, and to redistribute it to minimize surgical intervention. This method is called the intraoral blepharoplasty consists in abruption tissue through the mouth.

At the same time the doctor reaches the edge of the orbit, isolates “fat” hernia and sutured it to the soft tissues. It should be noted that this operation, due to its innovation, carried out by limited circle of specialists.

Recovery after blepharoplasty

The recovery period after blepharoplasty in Albuquerque is 10-12 days. In some cases, bruising are observed in the postoperative period, that persist up to 2 weeks.

Dr Ronald J Escudero Plastic Surgery For Eyes

In order to prevent their occurrence and to reduce swelling in the first few hours after blepharoplasty recommended use a cold compresses.

In any case, tissue swelling and bruising gradually disappear within 2-3 weeks. It is recommended to use eye drops for the first 3 days, as well as doing special exercises for the eyes.

Sutures are removed on the 3rd, sometimes 5-7 days after eyelid surgery.

The most common complication after blepharoplasty is the bleeding, which may occur in a few days and a few hours after the corrective eyelid surgery. So, often these operations are carried out on an outpatient basis.

During the first month do not recommend intensive loads that can cause additional bleeding due to increased blood pressure (including – hot showers, bend of the trunk, etc.).

During the first 2-4 months is necessary to observe the correct water-salt regime. All drug treatments should only be performed by agreement with your doctor.

The results of blepharoplasty

As a general rule, the final result of the cosmetic surgery under eyes is estimated in 1.5-2 months when the scar will be completely formed and postoperative edema disappear. The result can persist 7-10 years at observance of the recommendations specialists and proper care, and sometimes longer depending on the individual characteristics of the organism and the patient’s lifestyle.
