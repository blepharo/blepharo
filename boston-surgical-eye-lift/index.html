What is a blepharoplasty surgery?

Blepharoplasty is a surgical procedure on the lower and (or) the upper eyelids, aimed at improving their appearance and the face in general. Condition of the upper and lower eyelids always gives a person’s age.

The basis of the procedure is the removal of excess skin, redistribution or partial removal of intraorbital fat and lifting the circular muscles of the eye. Aesthetic blepharoplasty is one of the most popular operations in the world, due to its low cost and effectiveness.

The most common eyelid surgery is performed to men and women after 35 years old. Blepharoplasty is called one of the youngest operations, as the skin around eyes is very sensitive and susceptibility to the age-related changes.

Indications for blepharoplasty:

– Excess skin and its overhang in the area of upper eyelids;
– Ptosis of the upper eyelids;
– “Bags” under the eyes;
– Wrinkles in the lower eyelids;
– “Fatty hernia” of the upper eyelids;
– Ptosis lacrimal glands in the area of upper eyelids;
