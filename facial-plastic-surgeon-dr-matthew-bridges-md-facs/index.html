Can the bump on my forehead (osteoma) be removed?
Forehead osteoma
May 17, 2020
Forehead osteomas are relatively simple to remove.  I usually use an endoscopic approach to avoid an incision on the face.  The incision is made behind the hairline, and the endoscope is used to advance under the bone lining down to the mass.  The mass usually chips off and separates relatively cleanly from the bone underneath.  I have used a rhinoplasty rasp or a small spinning burr to smooth the edges.   It usually heals quickly with no visible scar.  Good luck!
