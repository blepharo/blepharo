The three main components of the eye lid operation are:

– removing excess stretched skin,
– strengthening muscles and ligaments,
– relocation or reduction in the volume of intraorbital fat.

Methods

Blepharoplasty is performed on the upper and lower eyelid. There are two ways to access of lower blepharoplasty: transconjunctival blepharoplasty and transcutaneous lower lid blepharoplasty:

1. Transconjunctival blepharoplasty. An Incision is made through the conjunctiva on the inner surface of the eyelids. Usually transconjunctival blepharoplasty allows to remove or to redistribute intraorbital fat, thereby to remove the hernia sac and to smooth the lacrimal groove.

The method is prescribed only for young patients with the presence of hernia sac, as it does not solve the problem of excess skin. It is also possible to combine the lower transconjunctival blepharoplasty with laser resurfacing of the lower eyelid (laser blepharoplasty).

This helps to reduce the number of wrinkles by reducing the lower eyelid skin after laser resurfacing. Eyelid procedure time is 30-40 minutes.

2. Transcutaneous blepharoplasty. The incision is performed on 1 mm below the lash line, on the outer surface of the lower lid by separating musculocutaneous flap. Thus access to the hernia of the lower lid is opened. Then intraorbital fat is removed or redistributed, depending on the individual patient. The excess part of the skin flap is removed and the wound is sutured with a cosmetic suture.

The scar becomes invisible after 2-3 months. Surgery to lift eyelids time is 1-1.5 hours. Usually during upper blepharoplasty in New Orleans, falcular strip of skin is excised and intraorbital fat is resected and sometimes muscle. The seam remains invisible because hidden in the natural crease of the eyelid.

The eye cosmetic surgery lasts 30 – 40 minutes. It is possible to perform canthoplasty or canthopexy (surgery to change the eye shape) in parallel with blepharoplasty, if necessary, or the patient’s desire.

Blepharoplasty is performed under local and general anesthesia. At the end of cosmetic surgery eyelids, on the eye is superimposed compressive bandage on 3 – 4 hours. Sutures are removed on day 3-4.

Contraindications.

There are severe systemic diseases, diabetes, coagulation disorders, glaucoma.

Side effects and complications

There are conjunctivitis, dry eyes, eversion of the lower eyelid, bleeding under the conjunctiva. Most complications are temporary.

The recovery period.

Swelling and bruising are disappearing after 2 – 3 weeks. Big sunglasses are very useful in this period. In the first few weeks, it is necessary to refrain from the use of contact lenses, sauna, sun exposure. A week later is permitted to apply makeup.

Actually, transconjunctival eyelid surgery, where the incision is performed on the mucosa of the lower lid, it makes sense to perform only up to 35 years old. This type of surgery eliminates the hernias, but not from the excess skin. Classical blepharoplasty in New Orleans is performed to remove wrinkles.
