Plastic Surgery On Eyelids In Singapore

Modern methods of of cosmetology and plastic surgery are used for rejuvenation of the eyelids.

Cosmetologist can help to improve the tone of the eyelid skin, improve firmness and get rid of facial wrinkles, but cope with the overhang of the upper eyelid, bags under the eyes can only plastic surgeon.

Surgical eye lift allows to obtain an excellent aesthetic and functional result after removal of bags under the eyes. Blepharoplasty, or eyelid surgery is a plastic surgery to remove excess skin and fat the upper or lower eyelid and sometimes strengthen their muscle tone.

Thanks to blepharoplasty in Singapore, you can get rid of bags under the eyes and swelling, some types of wrinkles, as well as to cope with such unpleasant disease like ptosis (lowering of one or both upper eyelids). It is possible to raise the corners of the eyes, to eliminate the asymmetry, remove excess skin on the upper and lower eyelids.

Blepharoplasty in Singapore is a lift of the upper and lower eyelids. As a rule, the above problems are beginning to emerge in 35-40 years old, but if there are emergence of individual predisposition, for example, fatty hernia, possibly at an earlier age.
