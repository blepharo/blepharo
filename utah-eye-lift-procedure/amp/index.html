Utah Eye Lift Procedure

Eyelids is a problematic area of the face, as the signs of aging are manifested early and clearly. From an aesthetic point of view, the eyes have great importance, because they are the center of attention, almost always.

Our interlocutors often look us in the eye. In the process involved the sensitive skin and deeper structures.

Age-related changes are manifested in the area of the upper eyelids in form of excess skin, on the lower eyelids is wrinkles and bags under the eyes.

Over time, eye lift using special exercise do not helps, but the surgery called blepharoplasty helps to effectively solve the problem.

Blepharoplasty in Utah is a procedure that allows significantly improve the condition of the eyelids and eye area by partial removal or redistribution of intraorbital fat, removing excess skin and lifting the circular muscle.

This direction in cosmetic surgery on eyes is one of the most popular.

What you need to know about blepharoplasty?

An eyelid surgery (Blepharoplasty) in Utah can be performed at any age. The upper and lower blepharoplasty can be done in the very young and in advanced age. Nevertheless, the majority operations performed for patients older than 35 years.

Since the skin of the eyelids is very thin and therefore heals quite quickly and easily. The scar usually passes on the folds of the eyelids, and therefore is almost imperceptible.

Eyelid surgery is performed in the presence of the following symptoms:
  1. drooping upper eyelids and lacrimal glands in their area;
  2. wrinkles or excess skin of the lower eyelids;
  3. bags under the eyes;
  4. muscle weakness;
  5. pronounced the nasolacrimal furrows.

Also, eye tightening surgery is indicated if the skin hangs in the upper eyelid, whereby the field of view is narrowed.

When these age-related changes will be corrected, the face will be look much younger and more attractive.

The area around the eyes is called periorbital area.

The manifestations of aging are divided into 4 types:

  1. the initial change in the area of a eyelid at which skin and muscle weakness are minimal and hernial protrusion is insignificant;
  2. the above manifestations are combined with excess skin on the eyelids, and age-related changes are already moving on the cheeks;

  3. changes in the area around the eyes, accompanied by sag eyebrows, the emergence of the eyelid-cheek furrow, deepening of the nasolabial fold and a pronounced of excess skin;
  4. profound changes, in which the lacrimal and eyelid-cheek furrows are clearly expressed, there are deep nasolabial fold and bags, as well as significant excess skin.

At the age of 35-40 are generally present only change the 1 type. In this case, surgeon removes only excess fat. In elderly patients often are present changes in the type 2-3.

It is necessary to remove excess skin for solve this problem, and sometimes muscle. When there is a change type 4 there is a need to facelift the midface.

Quite often I work with patients with drooping eyebrows. In this situation, a simple excision of the eyelid skin would give a short-term effect. Over time, the eyebrows have drop even lower, and the excess skin would arise again.

Contraindications to blepharoplasty

This operation is dangerous under certain conditions. They are including:

  • elevated intraocular pressure;
  • dry eye syndrome;
  • thyroid disease accompanied by expressed an exophthalmos;
  • problems with the cardiovascular system;
  • diabetes in the stage of development an expressed angiopathy;
  • neoplastic diseases;
  • a bleeding disorder.

Postoperative recovery

For blepharoplasty are characteristic a short period of rehabilitation and minimal probability of complications. Nevertheless, the first weeks of the recovery period may affect the result of the eyelid lifting.

At first you may experience some discomfort. This happens after any surgery. Swelling and bruising can cause discomfort, but after applying compresses and a special ointments they will disappear.

Until the seams will not be removed, in no case, do not touch the skin around the eyes, or to influence it in another way. Hygienic eye care will have to be carried out with extreme care, and from the use of contact lens is better to abstain.

You will be able to return to work after you’ll feel good, but exercise and heavy lifting, thermal procedures is necessary avoided for some time.

When you see the result of blepharoplasty?

The effect of the eye plastic surgery will be apparent for several weeks. Seam turns pale and eventually turn into an invisible line which securely hidden in the contours of a eyelid.

The result of the eyelid fold surgery is a long-term, but you must realize that your face will continue to age.

However, blepharoplasty is a good way to delay old age for a long time.
