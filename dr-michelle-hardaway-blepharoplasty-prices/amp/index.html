Scars with pinch blepharoplasty

Hypertrophic scars can be seen behind the ears after a facelift. It may occur from tension on the wound but can also even occur 6-12 months after the surgery. The skin of the lower lids is very thin and heals much differently than the thick skin behind the ears.

It would be very unusual to get a visible scar from a pinch of skin. If for some reason you do have a noticeable scar, injections of kenalog and 5-FU will usually eliminate the scar.

About 70% of my surgical practice is fixing prior eyelid surgery. Generally it is very difficult to revise and eyelid only 2 weeks after the original surgery. There is too much swelling and inflammation in very delicate tissues.

When to apply cold and warm after surgery

Cold application is good for the first 48 hours to minimize bruising and swelling. Cold will cause contstriction of the vessels to achieve this. After 48 _ 72 hours warm compress will increase the circulation to the area and may speed up the healing process but is not as effective as the cold application
