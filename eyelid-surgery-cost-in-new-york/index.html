Eyelid Surgery Cost In New York

Blepharoplasty in New York is a surgical method for the correction of skin and subcutaneous tissue in the periorbital region.

Eyelid surgery will eliminate any hereditary and acquired defects, bags under the eyes, overhanging or drooping eyelids, deep wrinkles and sagging skin.

Blepharoplasty of the lower and upper eyelids makes the eye a more open, creates an immediate effect of rejuvenation.

Indications for blepharoplasty

– Fatty bags of the upper or lower eyelid.
– Defects of the eyelids or severe asymmetry of the eye.
– The excess skin of the lower eyelid, upper eyelid overhang.
– Omitted corners of the eyes.
– Specific features: “heavy” or massive eyelids.
– The desire of the patient to change the shape of the eyes.

Contraindications to the eyelid surgery

– Chronic diseases in the acute phase.
– Diabetes, hormonal disorders.
– Acute infections, inflammatory processes.
– Diseases of the cardiovascular system.
– Problems with blood clotting.
– Eye and skin diseases.

Types of blepharoplasty

Depending on the indications for surgery distinguish three main types of blepharoplasty:

– The upper blepharoplasty is aimed at the correction upper eyelids (overhang, fatty herniation, etc.);
– Lower blepharoplasty resolves issues with the swelling and bags under the eyes;
– Circular blepharoplasty is used when it is necessary to completely change the shape of the eyes.

There are also operations of a narrow profile:

Canthopexy. The purpose of the operation to lift the outer corner of the eye. Wherein separate fibers of weakened muscles are attached to the periosteum of the eye. Canthopexy usually performed in parallel with the upper and lower blepharoplasty.

Asian blepharoplasty aimed at removing or mitigation of epicanthus (fold of skin, located between the inner corner of the eye and the bridge of the nose, most often found in people with Asian type of appearance) is visually lengthens eyes and gives them a European features.

Also, this operation allows to change the Asian shape of the eye and bring it closer to European standards. The operation can completely change epicanthus and to form an additional supraorbital fold.

The upper blepharoplasty

“Heavy” eyelids, overhanging eyelids, excess subcutaneous fat are the main indication for surgery. Often these physiological characteristics is the unattractive in appearance, but also adversely affect the vision – in the latter case, the operation is assigned by an ophthalmologist recommendations.

Upper blepharoplasty is performed under general anesthesia. Through small incisions (3-5 mm) in the natural crease of the eyelid, the surgeon removes the excess fat and restores the functionality of the ligaments and muscles.

Direct incision performed for excision of excess skin, which after healing is almost imperceptible and easily masked by cosmetics.

Lower blepharoplasty

Surgery of the lower eyelids is performed for cosmetic purposes – the operation helps remove the visual signs of aging of skin, bags under the eyes, swelling, herniation, wrinkles. There are two basic techniques:

Transcutaneous blepharoplasty in New York. The incision is performed along the lower contour of the eyelashes. Through it is performed excision of excess skin and fatty tissue. At the same time, surgeon can to correct the position of the muscles and strengthen it, and to tighten the skin and smooth out wrinkles.

Transconjunctival blepharoplasty. An incision or puncture are performed from the side of mucous membrane on the inside of the shell of the eyelid. This method has been successfully used for removing or redistributing fat tissue, but is powerless to sagging skin and wrinkles, so the appointed mainly for young patients.

Circular blepharoplasty. Simultaneous upper and lower blepharoplasty can solve several problems:

– Eliminate the visual signs of aging;
– To correct the asymmetry of the eye;
– Change the shape of the eyes;
– Get rid of wrinkles and fatty bags;
– To solve local problems (exophthalmos, entropion, etc.).

For patients whose primary purpose is to slow down the aging process and the “lose” 10-15 years old, the best choice would be exactly circular blepharoplasty.

The cost of the operation varies depending on the complexity of the procedures and volume.

Preparation for eye lid operation.

A few weeks before the expected date of the operation the patient must pass all necessary tests and to discuss in detail with your doctor course of the operation, and the desired result.

Compulsory tests:

  1. Blood: The general analysis and biochemistry;
  2. The presence of dangerous viruses (HIV, syphilis, hepatitis, etc.);
  3. Fluorography, ECG;
  4. Coagulogram.

Survey:

  1. eye exam, determining the presence of eye diseases;
  2. The volume the lacrimal fluid;
  3. A survey specialized professionals if there is a chronic diseases;
  4. Reactions to medications, anesthesia.
  5. In addition to consultations with your doctor, you must also visit the anesthesiologist to determine the type of anesthesia (general anesthesia or local anesthesia).

General rules for the preparation to blepharoplasty:

  1. You need to completely stop smoking. Nicotine significantly reduces tissue regeneration and increases the period of recovery.
  2. It is necessary to drink plenty of water and observe the daily routine.
  3. You have to give up beauty treatments, makeup, suntan (on the sun and solariums).
  4. Do not use any medication without approval of your doctor.
  5. A special prohibition applies to medicaments, which reduce blood clotting (aspirin, ibuprofen, etc.).

Selection of operation is based on a number of indicators:

  • The amount of excess fatty tissue;
  • The tone of the skin and cartilages;
  • The depth and severity of wrinkles or creases;
  • The volume of excess skin to excision;

Depending on the selected method of access there are following types of blepharoplasty:

Classical blepharoplasty. Correction of tissue is through surgical incisions along the anatomical folds (upper eyelid) or lash line (lower eyelid).
Transconjunctival. The incision is performed from the inside of the eyelid.
Laser blepharoplasty. The laser beam provides a local impact on the problem areas (suitable only in the early stages of skin aging).

Before the surgery on eyes, the surgeon makes marking on the skin and defines the area of impact. Then, the patient is administered an anesthetic, and begins the main part of the operation, it includes:

b) fat tissue redistribution (if necessary);
c) Lifting and strengthening the facial muscles (if necessary);

The operation takes a total of about one hour. Blepharoplasty in NY is one of the most popular operations. This is due to the minimal risk of complications and faster recovery period: all the “traces” disappear for a couple of weeks and you can return to the habitual life.

The postoperative period.

After general anesthesia, the patient must remain under medical supervision throughout the day. If the operation was performed under local anesthesia, the patient can go home a few hours later. In most cases, surgeons use a bioresorbable sutures.

In the case, if conventional sutures have been used, they are removed on the third or fourth day after the operation. A few days before surgery, the surgeon must submit a list of everything that you may need after blepharoplasty and recommendations for the use of each item.

All recommendations must be performed: creams and compresses applied to the skin, take antibiotics and do a dressing. A course is specifically designed for your speedy recovery.

The period of recovery:

  • Swelling and morbid sensations completely disappear in 2 – 2.5 weeks.
  • Bruises and other traces will going for 10 – 14 days. During this period, you need to sleep on back with your head elevated.
  • It is forbidden to use aspirin or other blood thinning medication.
  • It is desirable to refrain from alcohol and nicotine within a few weeks.
  • Within 10 days, you can not apply any cosmetic or lenses.
  • Within a month, you should avoid physical exertion, the hot procedures (sauna, steam bath) and exposure to the sun.

Some cosmetic procedures accelerate tissue repair (gentle peeling, massage, a special moisturizing).

Complications after the eyelid surgery.

The result of the operation depends largely on the skill and experience of the surgeon. The main thing for the patient is to choose a specialist, because the risk of various complications depends on him:

  1. Bruising, swelling, hematoma;
  2. Increased lacrimation;
  3. Doubling in eyes (diplopia);
  4. Incorrect scarring;
  5. Dry eye, conjunctivitis;
  6. Infection of the eye;
  7. Ectropion, blepharoptosis.

Fortunately, the unpleasant consequences such as ectropion or blepharoptosis are extremely rare. If the operation was performed professionally, all complications are reduced to bruising and swelling which accompany any surgical intervention, and disappear during recovery.
