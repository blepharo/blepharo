Cosmetic Surgery Eye Lift In Chicago

Blepharoplasty, or eyelid surgery In Chicago is a plastic surgery to remove excess skin and fat from the upper or lower eyelid and, in some cases, to strengthen their a muscular tone. Thanks to blepharoplasty can get rid of bags under the eyes and swelling, some types of wrinkles, as well as to cope with such unpleasant disease like ptosis (lowering of one or both upper eyelids).

It is possible to lift the corners of the eyes, to eliminate the asymmetry, remove excess skin on the upper and lower eyelids. As a rule, the above problems are beginning to emerge in 35-40 years old, but with the emergence of individual predisposition, for example, fatty hernia, possibly at an earlier age.

In addition, more and more popular becomes a so-called “Asian blepharoplasty” is an operation whose purpose is to change the eye shape and / or the formation of a “double eyelid”. This correction is often performed already in the 15-16 years.

Preparing for blepharoplasty

Eyelid surgery In Chicago requires not only great skill of the surgeon, but also a responsible approach on the part of the patient. Firstly you be required have a medical examination in order to identify possible contraindications.

Conjunctivitis, blepharitis, a syndrome of “dry eye” and glaucoma are require the preoperative treatment. Absolute contraindications are chronic diseases of the eye, any acute illness and severe myopia (nearsightedness).

Secondly – it is necessary to exclude the use of alcohol and some drugs that affect blood clotting, stop smoking and sunbathe, not later than 2 weeks prior to blepharoplasty.

How blepharoplasty is performed?

Surgery To Raise Eyelids For Asian Eyes In Chicago Illinois

Eyelid surgery is performed under local anesthesia. Perhaps the use of general anesthesia or other sedatives on the patient’s wishes. Upper eyelid blepharoplasty assumes the incision along the natural skin folds (palpebral crease), and then is performed the removal of excess skin and fatty tissue.

This operation is the most common and relatively simple. The incision is made under the lash line during the lower eyelid blepharoplasty. This is a very delicate and therefore much more difficult and responsible process – however, with proper execution of the baggy eyelid surgery, the result is quite impressive.

If necessary to remove a fatty hernia exclusively in the lower lid is possible to use transconjunctival blepharoplasty , in which an incision is made along the inside of the eyelid, allowing to hide the scar. For the success of such an operation requires a good elasticity of the skin of the patient, as a rule, its carrying out is possible only at a young age.

The work of the surgeon takes about an hour, after which the incisions are closed with stitches, sutures are closed with dressings and almost immediately you can go home, it is desirable, accompanied by someone from friends or relatives.

Recovery after blepharoplasty

In general, blepharoplasty In Chicago IL is characterized by a short period of recovery and minimal risk of complications.

Eye Tuck In Illinois Is A Favorable Procedure That Eliminates A Fatigued And Tired Look Common To Aging

However, the first week of recovery period after blepharoplasty may very significantly affect the result of the operation, so it is necessary to strictly comply with all recommendations of the surgeon, without exception.

Sutures are removed after 4-7 days after the cosmetic surgery on eyes, by this time post-operative bruising and swelling going down. Do not touch the skin around the eyes until removed the stitches. It is necessary to to abstain from the use of contact lenses and eye care will require a special accuracy. In addition, within a few days after blepharoplasty need absolute rest to eyes – should be excluded reading, TV and computer, as well as possible to avoid exposure to bright light (for the best effect is advisable to wear sunglasses for a further 2-3 weeks). It is unacceptable the use of alcohol, smoking and makeup.

After about 7-10 days, it will be possible to return to normal life, except the intense exercise, which are possible no earlier than 3 weeks after blepharoplasty. The final result of the operation can be estimated in 1-1,5 months, and the effect lasts for at least 5 (most often – on 7-10, in some cases up to 15) years.
