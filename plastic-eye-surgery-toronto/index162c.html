Plastic Eye Surgery In Toronto Canada

Blepharoplasty in Toronto is an operation aimed at improving the appearance of the eyelids and periorbital area (around the eyes), by removing excess skin, partial removal or redistribution of intraorbital fat and lifting the circular muscles of the eye.

The eyelids and periorbital region is one of the most prone to age-related changes the areas of the face.

A very delicate skin of eyelids more rapidly than any other gives out age of the man.

The changes affect not only the skin but also deeper structures. External manifestations are: excess skin in the upper eyelids, which can sag and even reduce the field of vision, wrinkles appear in the area of the lower eyelids and so-called “bags” under the eyes.

In such cases, the situation can be improved by using the operation called blepharoplasty.

Blepharoplasty is one of the most popular aesthetic surgeries.

An eyelid surgery can be performed at any age as long as a surgical indication is present.

Such an operation is possible to perform both in the young and in advanced age. The largest number of operations is done after 35 years old.

The skin of eyelids is the thin on human body surface, it gives the ability of more rapid healing as compared with other parts of the body.

In most cases, the scar are in the natural fold, and it practically impossible to discern after the procedure.

The main indications for blepharoplasty are:

  1. Overhang of the skin the upper eyelid;
  2. “Fatty hernia” of the upper eyelids;
  3. Ptosis (drooping) of the upper eyelids;
  4. Ptosis of lacrimal glands the upper eyelids;
  5. The excess skin of the lower eyelids;
  6. Wrinkles in the lower eyelids;
  7. flabbiness muscles;
  8. “Bags” under the eyes.

There are 4 types of age-related changes in the periorbital area.

Type 1 – the initial changes eyelids, with a minimal of muscular and dermal weakness, and minor herniation.

Type 2 – to the above changes are added, the excess skin the upper and lower eyelids, and the movement of age-related changes on the cheek.

Type 3 – the continuing changes the tissues periorbital area, with the sagging of the eyebrows, “skeletonization” the lower edge of the orbit, the appearance of the eyelid-cheek furrows and deepening of the nasolabial folds, and the excess skin are more pronounced.

Type 4 – pronounced changes, with the presence of pronounced lacrimal and eyelid-cheek furrows, excess fat, deep nasolabial fold, significant excess skin is also characterized by rounding the eye, in consequence of lowering the tone of the lower eyelid.

For each patient selected individual technique of the cosmetic surgery for eyes. There are cases when in the 35-40 age simply enough to remove excess fat through a transconjunctival access. This is possible when 1 type of age-related changes.

Patients older ages and with more pronounced changes (the type 2-3), as a rule, is necessary to remove excess skin and sometimes muscle, fat manipulation, lifting the eyebrows and canthoplasty. For patients with type 4 age-related changes is performed lower blepharoplasty and lifting of midfacial.

The usual result of blepharoplasty is a more open, cheerful and lively look, reduced overhang of the skin and swelling around the eyes. It is important to understand that the situation can be improved significantly in people with constantly swelling of the upper and lower eyelids. However, eyelid edema may persist after surgery.

It is also necessary to pay attention to the position of the eyebrows and to determine, not whether it is a cause of the excess skin on the upper eyelids. Quite often, patients have sagging eyebrows. As a rule, patients are not aware of it.

In such cases, by simple excision of the skin of the upper eyelids, achieved only short-term effect. The eyebrows lowered even lower and excess skin appear again.

During cosmetic surgery under eyes, the surgeon removes the skin and also can remove the muscles portion and a small amount of fat. At the end of the eye tightening surgery are superimposed cosmetic seams.

Blepharoplasty of lower eyelids

Before the operation the surgeon determines if there is excess skin on the lower eyelids, its elasticity, the presence of excess fat and a tone eyelids. If there is excess skin used transcutaneous lower lid blepharoplasty. When using this access – an incision is made in the skin, retreating 3-4 mm. from the lash line.

Adipose tissue is removed or redistributed of this area.Procedures such as the midface lifting, canthoplasty, canthopexy – and, if necessary, are made of this access. After that, excess skin is removed, and sometimes portions of circular muscles of the eye. On wound superimposed cosmetic seam.

Removing Heavily Wrinkled Skin Around The Eye

If there is no excess skin, performed transconjunctival blepharoplasty. Incision is made on the inside of the lower eyelid. Incision is closed with self-dissolving sutures or they will heal naturally without sutures.

This operation is effective in cases where there is excess fat in the area of the upper and lower eyelids.

During eye lid lift, the fat is partially removed or redistributed.
