Surgery For Eyelids In Nashville, TN

Blepharoplasty in Nashville, TN is performed in the presence of severe sagging and stretching of the skin, and as a result, swelling of the periorbital fat in some parts of the eyelids.

If necessary, blepharoplasty can be combined with tightening the forehead skin. Surgery of the upper and lower eyelids can be performed either separately or simultaneously.

Planning and carrying out of the surgery to lift eyelids

When planning a surgery for eyelids it is imperative to consult with your surgeon, giving him all the necessary information. If you wear contact lenses or glasses, be sure to take them for consultation.

The surgeon should describe the procedure in detail, explaining all the possible consequences and limitations. You can discuss with him all your questions.

Similar operations are implies intervention in the area of orbital fat. Therefore, before cosmetic surgery on eyes the patient must undergo a compulsory medical check-up and receive consultations of necessary specialists. At the end of surgery, the surgeon suturing the wound and apply bandages.

Indications for blepharoplasty

Typically, blepharoplasty is done to people (mostly women) aged 35 years and older, but nothing prevents you to solve their problems, and at a younger age.

What is the duration of the eyelid procedure?

Operation on the upper eyelids usually lasts about 40 minutes. Operation on the lower eyelids takes about 40-60 minutes.

Blepharoplasty Tennessee Before And After Photo

Upper Blepharoplasty is generally performed under local anesthesia, lower blepharoplasty is performed under general anesthesia.

Methods of surgical approaches.

When the operation on the upper eyelids, the incisions are in the crease of the upper eyelid, so that with open eyes postoperative scar is invisible. During the operation on the lower eyelids – incisions are just below the lash line.

Through these an incisions the surgeon separates the skin from the adipose tissue and muscle, removes excess fat, and often excised weakened skin and muscle. After that, the incisions are stitched with a cosmetic seams.

The possibilities of blepharoplasty.

During blepharoplasty in Nashville, TN is performed lift of the upper and lower eyelids, as a result of which it is possible to get rid of the age wrinkles, change the overall shape of the eyes. After eye lid lift, the eyes returned the erstwhile clarity sight, its openness and expressiveness.

In addition aesthetic correction, blepharoplasty is used to correct congenital defects (ptosis, or droopy eyelid) and acquired with age (the overhanging skin over the eyes that is hindrance to vision). After the cosmetic surgery eyes, not only the eyes looks younger, but also to the entire face.
