Denver Surgery Eyelid

Blepharoplasty in Denver is the operation, during which is performed lifting and removal of excess skin and fatty tissue of the eyelids, which helps to improve the appearance of eyelids, the elimination or reduction of aging changes around the eyes.

The result of blepharoplasty is the disappearance of “bags” under the eyes and wrinkles, puffiness, fresh and young look.

Blepharoplasty can be performed both in isolation and in combination with other aesthetic procedures and operations.

The eyelids are the most important anatomical structure of the human eye, protecting it from drying out, injuries, damage with the small mechanical particles, the impact of physical and chemical factors. The outer side eyelids is covered with the skin.

The eyelid is covered with a thin skin, the thickness of the dermis eyelid is less than 1 mm.

Circular muscle of the eye is located under the skin of the eyelids, is responsible for their mobility. A thin membrane separates the circular muscle from fat layer of the eyelids.

The basis of eyelids formed a cartilaginous plates. The innervation of the eyelids is carried out the branches of the facial nerve, woven into the circular muscle of the eye at its outer and lower edges.

The skin of eyelids is aging very early : after 30 years, it loses its elasticity, stretched and sag, because of what become visible fat deposits around the eyes.

Ptosis of tissues of the forehead and eyebrows have a negative impact on the appearance of the upper eyelids, giving the tired and heavy expression of the face.

Plastic surgery has developed and uses a variety of techniques for blepharoplasty, which are chosen based on individual complaints and the characteristics of each patient after a thorough review and analysis.

Types of eyelid surgery

Eyelid Surgery in Denver CO is performed on the lower and upper eyelids, and accordingly bear the name of the lower or upper blepharoplasty. During upper blepharoplasty are removed excess skin and fat on the upper eyelids, that causes it overhanging.

Lower blepharoplasty can be performed traditionally, with the removal of intraorbital fat or by saving it. Upper and lower blepharoplasty can be performed with transcutaneous or transconjunctival access.

Blepharoplasty is often supplemented with laser peeling eyelids to remove excess skin and fine wrinkles.

If there are significant age-related changes in addition to blepharoplasty can be performed periosteal or subperiosteal facelift. Canthopexy and canthoplasty are aimed at lifting corners of eyes and eye shape changes.

Blepharoplasty Is Generally Performed On An Outpatient Basis

Often canthopexy and canthoplasty are stages of blepharoplasty. There are two main methods of eyelid surgery to correct of the Asian type of eyes – with incision and without incision. When overhanging upper eyelid is performed correction of the upper eyelid (ptosis).

Preparing for blepharoplasty

Aesthetic indications for upper and lower blepharoplasty in Denver are excess skin on the upper eyelids, leading to the formation of skin folds and its overhang; the formation of fatty hernia lower eyelids (“bags under the eyes”) as a result of congenital structural features of the lower eyelids, or age-related changes.

The expected results of blepharoplasty patient discussing with a plastic surgeon at the preliminary consultation.

Denver, CO Blepharoplasty And Ptosis

The successful blepharoplasty allows achieving of more smooth contours around the eyes, reduce the number of irregularities – wrinkles, furrows, eliminate the overhang of the skin and as a result get more youthful and fresh look.

It is necessary to understand that with the help of blepharoplasty are not removed a temporary swelling around the eyes and small wrinkles and “crow’s feet”, although the situation can significantly improve after blepharoplasty.

It is also necessary to assess the position of the eyebrows, and in the case of a ptosis to decide on the conduct of combined operations – blepharoplasty and endoscopic facelift. If there is so-called “painting” bags is performed midface lifting in conjunction with blepharoplasty, and the elimination of pigmentation or mesh of wrinkles around the eyes is complemented by a laser or chemical peel.

Denver, CO Blepharoplasty Revision

Preoperative preparation for blepharoplasty involves a medical examinations and tests prescribed by a doctor (blood test, ECG, medical consultation, if necessary – consultation of narrow specialists), talk with the anesthesiologist to choose the right method of anesthesia.

Blepharoplasty in Denver usually performed in a short stay in a hospital under general anesthesia in conjunction with the local infiltration anesthesia and takes from 30 minutes to 1-2 hours.

The surgeon determines the optimal method of blepharoplasty individually for each case.

Today is possible to conduct blepharoplasty by methods, which exclude an external incisions and sutures, which allows achieves maximum aesthetic results.

Denver, CO Lower Lid Surgery To Improve Or Lift Droopy Eyelids

Incisions are placed along the natural folds of the eyelids, which makes them virtually invisible afterwards. Aseptic bandage is applied on the eyelids after blepharoplasty, which can be removed after 4-6 hours. Postoperative pain is expressed slightly.

The course of the postoperative period.

Removal of skin sutures is performed after 4-5 days after the blepharoplasty. Discharge from the hospital is performed in the evening on the day of surgery or the following day. Postoperative swelling of the tissues may persist up to 1-2 weeks, bruising for 5-8 days.

At this time, it is recommended to carry out a special hygienic care of the eye to prevent the formation of crusts, sleep with elevated headboard to reduce swelling tissues of the eye and to limit head movement.

Excess Upper Eyelid Skin Denver Surgery

After blepharoplasty in Denver is quite normal short-term decrease in sensitivity of the skin in the area of the lash line. The patient can return to work within 7-10 days at a favorable postoperative period. Since that time, are allowed the use of hypoallergenic cosmetics.

It is recommended to wear sunglasses that protect the eyes from ultraviolet radiation for 1-2 weeks after blepharoplasty. In the early postoperative period (2-3 weeks) are imposed restrictions on sport and physical activities.
