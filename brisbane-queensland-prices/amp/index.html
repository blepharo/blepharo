There’s No Scientific Data Saying That Warm Compresses Speed the Resolution of Bruising After Surgery; Cold Compresses Help!

In the first 24-48 hours following blepharoplasty surgery in Brisbane, we recommend cold compresses. Our primary concern during this time period is bruising and swelling. We feel that cold causes the small blood vessels in the eyelids to constrict
