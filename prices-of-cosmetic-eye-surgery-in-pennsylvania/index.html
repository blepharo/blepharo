Prices Of Cosmetic Eye Surgery In Pennsylvania

Blepharoplasty in Pennsylvania is the plastic surgery, which is performed to correct age-related changes in the eyelids.

Different methods of correction involves removing excess skin and fat of the eyelids, which is under them. In some cases, surgery of the muscles periorbital area can be performed.

Do not believe, that upper blepharoplasty can be perform without incisions – any plastic surgeon will tell you that this is impossible. After all, the essence of this correction is excision of excess upper eyelid skin, and cut out something without a trace is impossible.

So, the future result in this case depends on the shape and size of the excess skin, which will removed. The success of the eye lift operation depends on the future configuration of the eyelids, incisions and suturation.

Therefore it is impossible to carry out such operations routinely, without intelligence, taste and talent.

Otherwise, all eyelids after blepharoplasty will be identical. It should be understood that all eyelids are different – different shape of the eyes, the shape and position of the eyebrows and eyeball, the natural direction of wrinkles and folds in the skin …

The surgeon must feel all these nuances, then the results of blepharoplasty will be stable. Of particular importance is the formation of the incision and suture.

First, plastic eye surgery should be as accurate as possible, that comes with experience.

Secondly, the seam is superimposed accurately and beautifully – it is necessary perfect: put together the wound edges, and the suture material is used the thinnest.

Blepharoplasty 40 Year Old By Dr. Julio Clavijo-Alvarez

Third, removal of sutures is performed a single motion, for a few seconds.

For this, the seam originally should be formed “cross-cutting”. If the surgeon makes interrupted stitch , its removal takes a few minutes and disfigures result.

When properly executed operation and the absence of individual complications, the aesthetic appearance of the upper eyelids we get on the second week after plastic eye surgery.

Blepharoplasty (eyelid surgery) in Pennsylvania is an operation aimed at eliminating the overhang of excess skin on the upper eyelid, and getting rid of “bags” under the eyes. Essence of the operation (eyelid surgery) is the rejuvenation of the upper and lower eyelids by removing excess skin, fat and stretched muscle tissue.

Correct Eyelid Droop And Improve Your Overall Facial Appearance

Duration – About two hours.

Anesthesia – general or local anesthesia combined with intravenous sedation.

Hospitalization – Outpatient procedure.

Possible side effects.

This is a temporary discomfort, feeling of “tension” in the eyelids area, bruising and swelling. During the first few weeks after plastic surgery for eyelids may experience lacrimation and sensitivity to light. It can be also dryness, burning sensation or itching of the eyes (these phenomena are rare).

Possible complications.

Doctor Paul L. Leong Eyelid Rejuvenation

There are temporary blurred vision or doubling in eyes, infection, bleeding, in the corners of the eyes swelling, dry eyes, asymmetrical healing and scar formation, the temporary inability to squint, too low the position of the lower eyelid, which requires reoperation. Cases of irreversible vision loss is very rare.

The recovery period.

Patients can read books 2-3 days after eye cosmetic surgery and return to work in 5-10 days. Wearing contact lenses is permitted after 2 weeks after the eye lid lift surgery, but only after inspection of the surgeon.

After eyelid surgery in Pennsylvania, you can not drink alcohol. The loads and consumption of alcoholic beverages are possible 3 weeks after cosmetic surgery eyelids. Bruising and swelling disappear in a few weeks. The results are very individual in some patients persist for several years, others for life.
