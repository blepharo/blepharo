Surgery To Raise Eyelids In Seattle WA

Blepharoplasty In Seattle is a surgery aimed at correcting the state of the upper and lower eyelids (giving the expressive look).

The upper and lower blepharoplasty can be done both individually and comprehensively.

The reasons for performing this manipulation is age or genetically changes of the upper and / or lower eyelids, manifested in the form of excess skin (the overhang of the upper or lower eyelid and bags under the eyes) and fat herniation in this area.

Indications for blepharoplasty

It is excess skin and / or fat hernia eyelids.

Technology of blepharoplasty

According to the preoperative measurements and markings is performed excision of excess skin and fat removal of hernias.

On the lower eyelids can be perform septoplasty on the indications, canthopexy (strengthening the corner of the eye and the lower lid).

Postoperative scars are hidden under the upper eyelid in the natural folds of the skin, and on the lower eyelid is under lashes. Thus, the scars will eventually become almost invisible.

Anesthesia

Upper blepharoplasty surgery In Seattle can be performed under local or under general (optional patient) anesthesia. Lower blepharoplasty is performed under general anesthesia.

Duration of the surgical eye lift

Duration of lid blepharoplasty is 1-2 hours depending on the volume of correction required.

Hospital stay

Hospital stay is not required under local anesthesia. Hospital stay is 1 day when the upper and lower blepharoplasty surgery in Seattle performs under general anesthesia.

Postoperative period and recovery

  1. Slight pains, swelling and bruising are possible in the first days.
  2. It is removal of sutures in 4-5 hours.
  3. Restriction on the use of cosmetics up removal of sutures.
  4. You should avoid direct sunlight to prevent of pigmentation.

As after any surgery, you will be experience some discomfort, swelling and bruising, which will go down after the application of cold compresses and taking painkillers.
