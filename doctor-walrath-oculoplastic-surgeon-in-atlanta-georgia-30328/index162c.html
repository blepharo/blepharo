For Asian double eyelid incision procedure, is it necessary to have permanent fixation sutures burie
Suture material in double eyelid procedures.
Jan 27, 2019
Hi there,
Personally, I use permanent polyester sutures when fixating lid creases. The polyester is a braided suture, and some of your own tissue grows into it. It is not something that you feel or see. I choose permanent suture so that the crease can form properly and not regress over time. I use this same suture on perhaps 400 eyelids per year, and I cannot recall the last problem I have had related to it.
Best,
