Do I need a neck lift or lower facelift? Or both?
Lower face and neck lift
May 23, 2019
Weight gain can cause some laxity of the skin that may remain even after your 100lb weight loss. A combined lower face and neck lift will lift up the structures that have fallen down with gravity and remove the excess skin. Therefore it is best to combine the two. Just remember that even with this complete lift, there may be some lax skin, but your overall result should be excellent.

Normal swelling

All looks very good. Normal for the neck muscles to bulge a little for the first year due to swelling. With the tincture of time, all the swelling will likely resolve. Very nice result in my opinion!
Why am I starting to see Voluma lip filler bumps after my lips were reduced and refilled?
Voluma not ideal for lips
May 21, 2019
Voluma is not an ideal filler for the lips. The characteristics of Voluma as a filler are best used for deep injections. The complete opposite is generally needed for the lips. Some people may do well with Voluma to the lips, but if you have an issue with it, it is best to go to a different, less thick filler.
Would you recommend chin lipo or neck lipo?
Full neck Lipo
May 21, 2019
You seem to be an excellent candidate for full neck liposuction that will help sharpen the angle as well help define your jaw out to the sides as well. The fat liposuctioned from the neck will not be enough for the BBL or contribute to it significantly. It is important for your surgeon to do an exam and make sure you don't have any loose muscle or skin that I can not see on your photo.

Is it better to do the fillers after having a facelift has healed so as to better see where they m
Fillers one month after surgery
May 21, 2019
We age not only with gravity but with volume loss in our face. For best results we like to do lifts (facelift) to treat the gravity and volume replacement (fillers of fat transfer) together. Timing depends on personal preference. I usually will do fat transfer at the same time as the facelift. If we are choosing to use fillers, I usually wait one month after surgery (when the swelling has decreased) to more precisely place the filler.
3 weeks post-op. I can't open my mouth all the way. What is the usual outcome of a situation like
Lip asymmetry
May 21, 2019
At three weeks out from surgery, it is normal to have swelling that can lead to tightness and temporary inactivity of the nerves. However, it is important to follow up with your surgeon to fully assess the activity of your nerves and your skin. Sometimes the hibernation of nerves lasts a few weeks and sometimes can take longer. Follow up regularly with your surgeon to follow up and make sure there is improvement of the nerve function.

Could these stitches left in my ear post facelift be causing my pain and swelling?
Most likely not stitches causing pain
May 17, 2019
It is unlikely that this area is causing the pain that you are feeling. It is normal for some deeper stitches to come to the surface occasionally, and if they are dissolvable, will go away on their own. Sometimes we can get sharp pains and irrtitations, not because the nerves are effected, but because of the deeper normal healing process.
I tried Ultherapy and Botox. Am I out of options for my eyelids or can I try something else before
Surgery best
May 17, 2019
If Ultherapy and other light modalities haven't worked it would be best to consider and upper lid blepharoplasty. There is some new buzz with the plasma pen, but I do not have enough experience with it to comment fully on it. That being said, I would only recommend an upper eyelid lift for you.
1 month post op rhinoplasty: my tip is too high and my nose looks hooked. Will my tip drop?
Swelling takes one year + to go down
May 17, 2019
At this point it is normal to have significant swelling of the nose. It is common for the tip of the nose to look more upturned initially and will likely drop over the course of the year. Follow up with your surgeon to discuss steroid shots and taping the nose. Only after a year will you better be able to evaluate the effect of the hump removal.
