It is no secret that the indicators of age, in the first place are the hands, decolletage, as well as the eyes, more precisely, eyelids. But in some cases, for example, if the “bags” and swelling is too pronounced, the most effective cream and highly professional care will not give long-term results. The only correct solution would be plastic surgery.

What is a blepharoplasty?

Blepharoplasty in Richmond is a relatively simple, quick and effective procedure, having short recovery period. The result of this surgery would be the lack of wrinkles and “bags” under the eyes open and a radiant look. A great advantage of blepharoplasty is that post-surgical sutures are almost invisible even under closed eyelids because they are hidden in a fold of the upper eyelid or are on the mucosa the lower eyelid. Besides, they are very thin, not thicker than a hair, light lines.

Circular blepharoplasty in Richmond, when intervention is carried out on the upper and lower eyelids, but often the eye surgery is performed only on the lower or only on the upper eyelids. This depends on the condition of the tissue upon how pronounced defect.

Blepharoplasty of lower eyelids.

If the problem is only in the “bags” under the eyes and wrinkles, it is advisable to carry out transconjunctival blepharoplasty of the lower eyelids. This type of intervention involves a small incision on the inner, mucous membrane of the eye, while will not leave trace of the baggy eye surgery, and rehabilitation will be very fast.

Blepharoplasty of upper eyelids.

The upper blepharoplasty in Richmond VA is performed in cases when there is an overhang of the upper eyelid. This operation can be classic and endoscopic. In classical surgery, the surgeon makes an incision in the crease of the upper eyelid, a centimeter above the lash line, removes the excess skin and the eyelid returns to the ideal position.

During endoscopic blepharoplasty, the surgeon makes small punctures in the scalp and performs simultaneous lifting eyelids and eyebrows. This operation has the effect of which can be compared with the effect of a full facelift: a complex of signs of aging eliminated, a look completely “open”.

It should be noted that, any method of blepharoplasty may be supplemented by one-stage laser rejuvenation of the skin around the eyes. This will help to provide more visible results, because the quality of the skin is much improving, it will become more smooth, supple.

Contraindications to blepharoplasty.

Like any surgical procedure, blepharoplasty has certain contraindications. Blepharoplasty can not be done during pregnancy and lactation. It is contraindicated if there is malignancies and severe blood diseases, and infectious exacerbations of any chronic diseases, systemic lesions internal organs and, if there is a serious ocular disease. This surgery can not be performed in childhood and adolescence.

The recovery period.

The recovery period is always lasts comfortable. Pains after blepharoplasty are absent, can only be swelling and minor bruising, but they disappear in a single week.

You can use sunglasses and even apply makeup to hide residual traces of the plastic eye surgery.

During the first month after eyelid surgery, it is desirable to abandon swimming and any sport, as well as visiting the baths, sauna and solarium. You should also protect yourself from UV rays, but not necessary to change dramatically climatic zone. Initial results after surgery will be seen after 2 weeks. And when the tissue is completely healed in the new position (it will happen in 1-2 months), the eyes will look perfect.
