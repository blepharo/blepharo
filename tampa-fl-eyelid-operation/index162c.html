Alas, we are all getting older, and the signs of aging are most noticeable on the face. Over time, on the eyelids appear wrinkles, bags under the eyes are forming.

The effect of the use of creams is almost imperceptible. Injectables and laser treatments can solve a problem mainly with getting rid of wrinkles.

If your look has ceased to please you and you want to change it for the better – there is reason to consult an expert and learn how to achieve the desired changes.

Eyelid surgery (upper and lower eyelids) in Tampa FL refers to anti-aging operations, but indications for eyelid surgery can be observed in younger patients.

Tampa blepharoplasty is not limited by age range and is performed when indicated. Excess skin, hernia of the lower and upper eyelids are encountered in young people, the reason for it is heredity or ethnic identity.

Surgical correction of eyelids involves the removal of orbital fatty tissue usually along with excess skin and muscle (if indicated) in the upper and lower eyelids. The goal of surgery is to remove the overhanging upper eyelids and “bags under the eyes” – the age signs that make you older, giving the face tired look and sometimes create obstacles eyesight.

At the same time it should be noted that the surgery on the eyelids do not eliminate the “crow’s feet” in the outer corners of the eyes and other small wrinkles and the dark circles under the eyes, do not change the position of the eyebrows.

Some an esthetic problems can be eliminated by other operations, such as facelift. With the help of blepharoplasty in Tampa is possible to deepen the crease in the upper eyelid area or get rid of folds covering the inner corner of the eye, but still surgery does not eliminate ethnic affiliation.

Changing eye shape is not the task of rejuvenating eyelid surgery, the possibility of this kind of correction is discussed with the surgeon. Blepharoplasty can be done as a separate operation, or be combined with other rejuvenating operations on your face.

Some patients, when faced with the necessity of surgical correction of facial and periorbital areas eyelids, refuse the procedure, considering it a painful, inefficient and costly.

Types of eyelid surgery

1. The upper eyelid blepharoplasty (removal of sagging skin, lifting and correction of eyebrows, change the “Asian eyes” – epicanthus correction);
2. Lower eyelid blepharoplasty (removal of dark circles and “bags” under the eyes, correction of puffiness and sagging of periorbital area);
3. Сircular blepharoplasty upper and lower eyelids;
4. Transconjunctival blepharoplasty.

The advantages of eyelid surgery

The beauty of the eye is a phenomenon that subject to “magic” of cosmetic surgery, has reached incredible success in the application of radio frequency and laser technology.

Blepharoplasty can help get rid of age-related changes, correct the defect bulging eyes (exophthalmos), to correct the location of the fat and rejuvenated appearance.

We offer traditional and alternative technology the eyelid surgery, which include:

  1. classic – radical blepharoplasty (incision perform with a scalpel or a laser beam);
  2. termolifting (using radio waves or infrared rays to enhance the biological processes in the deeper layers of skin);
  3. Electrosurgical Blepharoplasty (utilizes a high density radio frequency energy between 5 and 2.5 MHz.)
  4. Laser eyelid surgery.
