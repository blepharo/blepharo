Cosmetic Eye Surgery In United Kingdom

In vain, many believe that eyelid surgery (blepharoplasty) is a fairly simple operation.

On the contrary, blepharoplasty in UK can be considered one of the most difficult surgeries in cosmetology, as any, even the slightest inaccuracy or surgeon’s error becomes unbearably noticeable to others, causing the most dramatic experiences of the patient.

And this is understandable because any acquaintance we begin with eye contact. Suffice it to recall that already in childhood man can express the most varied feelings with the help of an eye.

Blepharoplasty is divided into plastic surgery upper and lower eyelids. Very often, these two operations are carried out simultaneously.

The common opinion that with the help of blepharoplasty can remove wrinkles outer corners of the eyes (“crow’s feet”), is an erroneous.

This requires additional surgical procedures, the volume of which is determined by the peculiarities of each case.

Indications for blepharoplasty

The absolute indication for blepharoplasty in the United Kingdom is the presence of excess skin. This is manifested by the presence of additional wrinkled folds of skin in the upper eyelids area, which gives a gloomy the facial expression.

Often the reason which prompted the patient to see a doctor, is the usual inconvenience in applying make-up and increased eye fatigue – excess skin of the upper eyelids interfere to see normally what is usually compounded by the evening.

Unfortunately, ordinary, even a very qualified skin care, do not prevent the formation of skin excess in the upper eyelids, as the skin there is very thin, contains almost no subcutaneous fat and, therefore, constantly being reduced and relaxing during a blink, inevitably stretched.

Hereditary factors are played an important role. With the problems of excess skin and “bags” on the eyelids turn to us and very young people, twenty-five – thirty years. Depending on the shape and position of the eye sockets, the characteristics of the facial skeleton and ethnic factors, properties of skin, muscle layers, skin folds can be located along the century or in the outer corners of the eye, forming a kind “house”.

In addition, also owing to the age, or ethnic factors problem of the upper eyelids is reflected in the formation of subcutaneous fat hernias, which are particularly noticeable in the inner corners of the eyes. It’s easy to check the presence of these hernias without a doctor – a light finger pressure on the eyeball leads to bulging of the skin in the area of the interior angles of the upper eyelids.

Excessive protrusion of fat hernia is a sufficiently clear indication for blepharoplasty.

How is eyelid surgery performed?

Blepharoplasty in UK is a low-traumatic and usually quite easily tolerated by patients. The operation is performed local anesthesia. After the pre-marking of the skin, is performed excision excess skin followed by removal excess fat lumps, which are located under the muscle.

There are a lot of nuances in the course of the operation, a thorough compliance with which could lead to the ideal result.

Double Eyelid Surgery Day 3 United Kingdom, England Candidate

For example, sometimes there are aesthetic limitations in case too radical removal of fatty hernia – to avoid a “failure” under the eyes, we reserve a certain amount of adipose tissue, in this case, some swelling will look better than the “sunken” eyes.

Blepharoplasty lasts about 1.5 hours. The incision line on the lower eyelid passes directly under the eyelashes, fatty hernia removed after careful detachment of tissues. After that, the skin is placed on the place, and there comes the crucial moment of operation, which is the excision of excess skin. If the skin will be excised not fully, the post-operative effect will be incomplete.

If we allow excessive skin excision possibly expansion eye slit, and in extreme cases – eversion of the ciliary edge of the lower eyelid. On the upper eyelids is excised skin so that the postoperative scar was placed in the natural crease the eyelid. Determination of the true number of removed skin on the eyelids and is an indication of the art the surgeon.

Sutures are removed on the 4th day after the operation to create conditions conducive to optimal healing and scar formation. You should not scare the usual bruising and swelling in the eye area – about a week after blepharoplasty they finally disappear with the full care and treatment. The scar is in a natural depression in the upper eyelids and therefore invisible to others.

Sometimes a cause for concern are some difficulty closing the eyelids and redness the scar, but it’s easy to goes through several weeks after plastic eye surgery. In particularly difficult cases, such as increased bleeding or the tendency to the formation of edema, for the final stabilization of the postoperative picture may require 3-4 months.
