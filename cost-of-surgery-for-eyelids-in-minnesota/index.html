Cost Of Surgery For Eyelids In Minnesota

Blepharoplasty in Minneapolis is a method of correction of age-related changes periorbital (periocular) area.

Blepharoplasty involves:

– surgery (rejuvenation) of the upper eyelid;
– surgery (rejuvenation) of the lower eyelid with the removal of fat herniation or without it;
– change the shape of the century (interracial transitions).

After blepharoplasty in Minnesota, look becomes more open and the young, and the eyes appear larger.

Indications for blepharoplasty

– overhang of the skin of the upper eyelids;
– “Bags” under the eyes (fatty hernia);
– excess skin in the lower eyelids area;
– “Sunken” eyes (“blueness” under the eyes) with pronounced nasolacrimal grooves.

Preoperative preparation

Preoperative preparation includes the mandatory consultation of the plastic surgeon, in which you discuss your wishes. Also, the doctor will advise you about the volume of the operation. Before blepharoplasty, it is necessary to pass a standard inspection is tests, ECG, in some cases, may need to consult an ophthalmologist and endocrinologist. Immediately before the operation is made photography, to could be compared results before and after cosmetic under eye surgery. Blepharoplasty in Minnesota is performed under general and local anesthesia combined (at the same time various methods of general and local anesthesia are used).

A plastic surgery for eyes lasts about 1 hour.

The postoperative period (recovery)

After eyelid uplift, the sutures are removed on the 5th day. After that, you can already use cosmetics. At first, you might be concerned by bruising and swelling, which are usually completely resorbed at 12 – 14 days. You will be able to return to your normal lifestyle within 1 – 2 weeks.
