How is eyelid surgery performed?

There are a number of aesthetic blepharoplasty techniques. Methods of operations may differ greatly in different situations, depending on the prevailing conditions, the severity of the signs of aging, excess skin and fatty tissue, and the surgeon’s decision.

Classical techniques of blepharoplasty involve incisions upper and lower eyelids, which are located along the natural folds and removal of excess skin and fatty tissue protrusion. Incisions are carefully sutured with a cosmetic suture.

The new method of surgery of the lower eyelids, the so-called transconjunctival blepharoplasty appeared with the development of cosmetic laser technology. This method allows to carry out an operation without external incisions and without stitches.

When expressed skin surplus in the lower eyelids simultaneously performed laser resurfacing skin infraorbital areas that allows to eliminate not only the excess skin and epidermal wrinkles, pigmentation disorders and to achieve skin rejuvenation in this area.
