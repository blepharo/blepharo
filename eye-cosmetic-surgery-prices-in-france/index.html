Malar bags and Lower Blepharoplasty

Malar bags are anatomically separate from the lower eyelid. Lower blepharoplasty in France does not correct malar bags, and no procedure reliably does. On the other hand, lower blepharoplasty, like any injury to the area, may temporarily increase edema in the malar bags

The key word here is temporarily; as the surgical swelling resolves, so should any increase in malar bags.

A lower lid blepharoplasty in France is designed to remove the lower lid bags and if present remove some excess skin. Malar bags are a big problem — but are not caused by the surgery.
