The term “blepharoplasty” most of us are not familiar. Most often we can hear another name for this plastic surgery: eyelid surgery. It is worth noting that blepharoplasty today enjoys enough popularity. Women and men of different ages are facing this problem.

Why blepharoplasty is so popular?

The answer to this question is quite simple: the first signs of aging appear around the eyes, and we all want to look young and healthy as long as possible. It’s no secret that the eyes are involved in facial expression.

Expression of eyes is determined by the state of the eyelids. The loose skin around the eyes, “bags” under the eyes or hanging folds of the upper eyelids these things are poisoning the lives of millions of people. Your appearance will hopelessly spoiled if your eyes are surrounded by swollen circles or grid wrinkles.

So, it is eyelid surgery (blepharoplasty) In GreensboroÂ allows to eliminate some of the signs of aging, as well as to correct changes that cause the effect of “tired” look.

The main indications for blepharoplasty are:

– overhanging skin the upper eyelids, which may cause narrowing of the visual field;
– “Fatty hernia” of the upper eyelids;
– ptosis of the upper eyelids;
– ptosis, lacrimal glands in the upper eyelids;
– excess skin of the lower eyelids;
– wrinkles in the lower eyelids;
– flabby muscles;
– “Bags” under the eyes;
– deep nasolacrimal grooves.

And if in the first case – bags under the eyes – the problem lies in the sprawling adipose tissue surrounding the eyeball, in the second case – the excess skin of the upper eyelids is the inherited or acquired cause.

Therefore, modern cosmetic blepharoplasty involves not only the removal of excess skin, and includes a different methods for eliminate age-related changes of the periorbital area.

There are 4 types of age-related changes in the periorbital region.

Type 1 – the initial changes of the eyelids, with a minimal the muscle and skin weakness, and minor hernia protrusion.
Type 2 – the excess skin in the upper and lower eyelids and and displacement of the age-related changes to the cheek are added to the above changes.
Type 3 – tissue changes of the periorbital area, with the sagging of the eyebrows, “skeletonization” the lower edge of the orbit, the appearance of the eyelid-cheek grooves and deepening of the nasolabial folds, and the excess skin is more pronounced.
Type 4 – pronounced changes, with the presence of pronounced lacrimal and eyelid-cheek grooves, the protrusion of fat, deep nasolabial fold, the presence of bags, significant excess skin, round eyes, as a consequence of lowering the tone of the lower eyelid.

Lower blepharoplasty (lower eyelids surgery).

Before the eyelid drooping surgery, the surgeon determines the presence of excess skin on the lower eyelids, its elasticity, the presence of excess fat and tone of the eyelid. If there is excess skin – the surgeon uses a transcutaneous access. When using the transcutaneous access – an incision is made on the skin, retreating 3-4 mm. from the lash line. Fatty tissue is removed,redistributed or added to this area when its deficit.

Procedures such as midface lifting, canthoplasty, canthopexy, if necessary, are performed of this access. After that, excess skin is removed, and sometimes portions of circular muscles of the eye. Cosmetic seam is superimposed on wound. Transconjunctival blepharoplasty is carried, if there is no excess skin.

The position incision and the details of the plastic surgery for eyes:

For the upper eyelid: an incision is performed on the crease eyelid; for the lower eyelid: an incision passes directly under the eyelashes or the inner side of eyelid.

Recovery:

The first week is not recommended to excessively strain your eyes (to read a lot, watch TV, work on the computer). The stitches on the eyelids are removed on day 4-5. You can go back to work after 1 week. After 3 weeks is allowed to renew the previous physical activity.

Swelling and bruising usually disappear within 7 – 14 days. The result is saved for a long-time, depending on the patient’s general aging rate.
