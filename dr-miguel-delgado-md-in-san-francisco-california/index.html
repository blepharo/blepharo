My glands are still visible, is it possible to fix with a new surgery and also remove the gland tota
Gynecomastia Revision Surgery
Jan 27, 2020
Liposuction alone will only treat pseudogynecomastia which is gynecomastia caused only by fat. Most men have true gynecomastia which is a combination of fat and breast tissue. If liposuction alone is done a revision will need to be performed to remove the breast tissue, this will be trickier as now there is scar tissue involved. 40% of my gynecomastia practice is revision surgery. Consult with a board certified plastic surgeon that specializes in gynecomastia. Good luck.
