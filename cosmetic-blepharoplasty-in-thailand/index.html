Cosmetic Blepharoplasty In Thailand

Age-related changes of the face are manifested primarily in the eye area and neck. Therefore, eyelid surgery, or blepharoplasty, in Thailand is the most popular plastic surgery in the facial area.

It is applied if there are “bags under the eyes”, large and small wrinkles on eyelids, to eliminate the effect of “sagging” eyelid.

Bags under the eyes can be removed with a conventional method (using a scalpel) or with innovative laser technologies. In our clinic we use both methods.

The plastic surgeon will determine the best way to solve the aesthetic problem in each case during free consultation.

The operation to remove bags under the eyes is performed both on the upper eyelids (upper blepharoplasty) and lower eyelid (lower blepharoplasty).

Circular blepharoplasty performed if there are fatty deposits or sagging skin on both eyelids. The operation involves the removal of excess fat, excision sagging skin and applying special microsurgical sutures.

After the end of the rehabilitation period, incisions are virtually unnoticeable, the skin of eyelids becomes elastic.

Blepharoplasty is an effective and minimally invasive procedure in which tissue dissection and removal of fatty deposits is done with meticulous precision. The procedure may be performed at any age.

  1. Removing aging skin changes of eyelids;
