Las Vegas Plastic Surgery Eyelids

Blepharoplasty in Las Vegas is the plastic surgery, which aims the removal of excess skin and fatty tissue (partial excision of muscle, if indicated) in the upper and lower eyelids. In simple terms, blepharoplasty effectively solves the problem of bags under the eyes, and eliminates the excess skin and fatty tissue on the upper eyelids.

A look after blepharoplasty becomes younger and more open. Age: Eyelid surgery is often performed for people older than 35 years, for the purpose of correction of age changes face.

Young people are less frequently resort to blepharoplasty, aesthetic shortcomings of eyelids are directly connected with heredity or ethnicity.

Las Vegas blepharoplasty significantly improves the appearance of the eyes, boosts self-confidence. But at the same time, possibilities of blepharoplasty are not limitless.

Your expectations from the surgery on eyelids should be discussed in detail with the plastic surgeon. The best candidates for blepharoplasty are men and women with pronounced aesthetic defects of the upper and / or lower eyelid – bags under the eyes, excess skin and hernia of the upper eyelids.

The age of patients, in the majority of cases starts between 30 years and older.

How the skin of eyelids grows old?

Over time, skin of eyelids loses its elasticity , the number of folds increases in the upper eyelids area, increasing the severity and number of crow’s feet at the outer corner of the eye, bags under the eyes are formed.

These signs of aging caused by the skin eyelids structure, because the thin and delicate skin more subject to the influence the aging process. Most people have already wrinkles at the age of 20-30 years, due to the reduction of circular eye muscles.

This is the first expression wrinkles in the outer corners of the eyes, or “crow’s feet”. In addition, the deep-lying structures susceptible to change – the muscle is stretched with age, under-eye bags appear.

Fatty tissue protrudes outwards due to the relaxation of the orbital septum are formed “hernias” in the upper and lower eyelids area. Often the excess skin on the upper eyelids are a hindrance vision or impede the applying of makeup.

It should be noted that bags under-eye are sometimes formed due to certain diseases and can not be eliminated by plastic surgery for eyes.

Eyelid swelling may occur due to problems with the kidney, thyroid, due to allergic reactions, and several other diseases. In preparation for the cosmetic surgery under eyes, all these aspects are discussed in detail with the plastic surgeon.

Preparation for surgical eye lift:

  1. 10 days before the blepharoplasty is recommended to exclude the use of alcoholic beverages;
  2. It is recommended a temporary cessation of smoking;
  3. 10 days before the blepharoplasty should stop taking medications that affect process of blood clotting;
  4. if the cosmetic eye lift is performed under general anesthesia, then you should refrain from eating before corrective eyelid surgery for at least 10 hours;
  5. before the bottom eyelid surgery face is thoroughly cleaned of any traces of cosmetics.

Anesthesia: eyelid surgery can be performed under local or general anesthesia.

The duration of cosmetic surgery eye lift is from 1 to 3 hours, depending on the complexity, the volume of the forthcoming dropped eyelid surgery. Hospital stay: when performing blepharoplasty under local anesthesia, as a rule, the patient can go home on their own after a few hours. When the cosmetic eye lift performed under general anesthesia – the patient is in the hospital one night.

If blepharoplasty in Las Vegas NV was performed as part of a larger intervention (facelift), then the patient’s stay in hospital can be extended up to 2-3 days.

Sutures are removed after 3-5 days after the eyelid surgery, after which edge incision is fixed with special stickers, which the patient removes about a week after cosmetic under eye surgery.

  2. 2-3 weeks should avoid wearing contact lenses;

Early postoperative period which is characterized a temporary discomfort, tension sensation, tissue swelling, bruising of the eyelids, as well as temporary dryness, burning and itching in the eyes.

Also possible are: increase lacrimation, sensitivity to light the first few weeks after blepharoplasty correction.

The recovery period. Prolonged reading is recommended to renew after 2-3 days, return to work after 10-14 days after eye bag removal surgery, contact lens wear can be resumed for at least 2 weeks after eye drooping surgery.

Physical activities, including playing sports is permitted in about 3 weeks. Bruising and swelling go down through 2-3 weeks (individually for each patient). The result of blepharoplasty is estimated not earlier than 2 months after eye lid lift surgery.

Saving the result: as a rule, result remains for a long period of time, but nevertheless does not eliminate the natural aging process.

Painful sensations are mild. In most cases, intake of painkillers is not required after blepharoplasty.
