Is it okay to get blepharoplasty before weight loss is complete?
Is it okay to get blepharoplasty before weight loss is complete?
Jan 13, 2019
The lower eyelid fat pockets tend to be diet resistant areas. Additional weight loss should not significantly affect the fat pockets but can impact the surrounding tissues in this region. If your surgeon is considering fat transfer, which is often performed with the lower eyelid surgery, then you may want to consider waiting until you are at a stable weight.

Do you have to take antibiotics for a lower face lift?
Do you have to take antibiotics for a lower face lift?
Jan 13, 2019
Antibiotics are not necessary after a facelift. The risk of infection is very low following this type of surgery and studies have not demonstrated a clear advantage. In my practice that focuses on facial rejuvenation we give one dose of antibiotics through the IV prior to facelift surgery, but no other antibiotics are necessary.

The risks associated with general anesthesia are related to your age, your overall health status and the extent of your procedures. Which surgery do I choose between: rhinoplasty or septoplasty ?
Septoplasty vs Rhinoplasty
Jan 11, 2019
A septoplasty is a procedure to straighten the inside cartilage of the nose. This generally does not affect the external appearance of the nose but does improve the inside opening of the breathing passages. A rhinoplasty is generally considered a procedure to address the outer appearance of the nose and is frequently performed for cosmetic reasons.
I hit my nose (post rhino) on the edge of my bed headboard. Should I be concerned ?
Injury After Rhinoplasty
Jan 11, 2019
The nose can be sore for a few weeks following your rhinoplasty procedure. Despite your best efforts to avoid any injury to the nose, events like tis can happen. Any injury to the nose during healing will make it more sensitive. It is always safest to have your nose evaluated by your surgeon who performed the procedure and they can assure you if the nose has not been affected.
