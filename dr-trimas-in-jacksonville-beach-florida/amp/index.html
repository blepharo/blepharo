If you remove fat, this can result in hollowing of your upper eyelids or a change in your eyelid skin. If you just remove excessive skin, then you can avoid hollowing effects.

Is it possible to get polly beak deformity and nose thinning from a rhinoplasty?
Causes of poly beak abnormality
Jun 10, 2020
There are many causes of a poly beak issue after rhinoplasty. It is possible to leave too high of a septum in the supra tip region or not remove enough cartilage there. Sometimes scar tissue can build up in that area and create a supra tip fullness hence a poly beak deformity. Other reasons include too heavy of a nasal tip can lead to drooping and a poly beak issue. I would recommend a board certified facial plastic surgeon in your area for further evaluation.
