Should you let Botox Wear off before Upper Blepharoplasty surgery?
Upper Blepharoplasty & Botox
Dec 27, 2019
Botox can impair your surgical results if you have undiagnosed brow ptosis. If your surgeon is aware of your brow ptosis and is addressing it during your upper eyelid surgery it most likely will not affect your results. How can you make sure your surgical results aren’t compromised? Be sure to remind/tell your surgeon you have had Botox so that they can take it into account before they take you to surgery.
