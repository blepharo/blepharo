Does BroadBand Light get rid of flat, dark moles?
BBL and moles
Jun 23, 2020
BBL will not get rid of moles. BBL will treat pigmented lesions that are due to sun exposure. This kind of pigment is relatively superficial and can be corrected with lasers. A mole is usually pigmented quite deep and the BBL won't be able to get rid of that much pigment, at least not without multiple treatments, and even then, there would be no guarantee. There are other options, but the mole would need to be personally evaluated to discuss them.Andrew Campbell, M.D.Facial Rejuvenation ...
