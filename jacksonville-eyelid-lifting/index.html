Jacksonville Eyelid Lifting

Beautiful and expressive eyes were considered the attribute of a beautiful face at all times.

This is not surprising. Our eyes play a major role in communication. When we look into the other person’s eyes, we involuntarily pay attention to the eye view.

But new problems come with age. There are sympathetic comments others,about the tired eyes, and the man himself notices bags under the eyes and heavy eyelids.

Indications for Eyelid surgery in Jacksonville.

– Excess skin of the upper and / or lower eyelids;
– Fatty herniation of the upper and / or lower eyelids;

Eye tiredness due to sagging skin of the upper eyelids, that interferes with normal vision. Excess skin give the face a heavy and tired look. Excess skin of eyelids can be seen without any manipulation.

The skin fold on the upper eyelids may overlap the lash line, and sometimes part of the eye slit at the outer corner of the eye (blepharochalasis). At the same time skin is thinned and stretched to the form of parchment paper. Such signs are detected and on the lower eyelid, the skin can be stretched down to the lacrimal groove.

The eyeball is surrounded by a layer of fat. However, the fat protrudes in the eyelids with age, creating swelling. This process is not related to obesity and weight loss will not help to cope with it. It is caused by aging and heredity.

This swelling can not be removed with the help of cosmetic procedures. Removing excess fat is only possible surgically.

Before And After Blepharoplasty In Jacksonville, Florida Photo

To help understand whether there is fat or not, close one eye and careful press on another, through the closed eyelid. If when pressed, the skin bulges out above the eye and under the eye, the cause is excess fat.

Blepharoplasty in Jacksonville is an operation aimed at restoring aesthetics, conservation and / or restoration of function of eyelids. It is a highly individualized surgery. Only the surgeon can choose the volume and the method of its implementation.

Blepharoplasty lower eyelids.

Lower eyelids surgery in Jacksonville FL is also performed using an incision on the skin, receding from lash line. The surgeon removes the fat with excess skin that accumulates under the muscle.

Blepharoplasty Week 3 Florida Image

Blepharoplasty of lower eyelids with the transconjunctival incision.

If the patient has fatty hernia in the projection of the lower eyelid, without excess skin, then the operation is performed without an incision on the skin. The incision is made inside the eyelid, through the conjunctiva (mucous membrane of the eye).

This technique allows to remove the excess hernias only the lower eyelid, without an incision on the skin, without sutures and thus reducing the duration of the operation.

Preparation for eyelid hood surgery:

Consultation with the surgeon. Patients have pass the tests in the clinic, the validity of blood tests 10 days.

Eyelid repair surgery:

The patient comes to the Jacksonville clinic for eyelid reduction surgery, concludes an agreement on the treatment and makes the payment. The patient is in the ward. The surgeon makes the markup and the photo to compare the results after eye fat surgery.

Anesthesia: local anesthesia, intravenous anesthesia. The duration of eye sagging surgery is 30 to 90 minutes. After eye lift operation, the patient is in the ward 2 to 5 hours. Perhaps stay in hospital during the day, if necessary.

Postoperative treatment:

Male Eyelid Surgery In Jacksonville, FL Pics Before And After

Dressings will be performed on an outpatient basis (you will need to come to the clinic), about 2-4 visits. Sutures are removed on 2-5 day, if the eye drooping surgery is performed using a transconjunctival incision, the sutures are not removed.

Swelling and bruising disappear after 3-14 days, depending on the volume of operations. If only the skin was removed, the swelling go down through 3-5 days. The first few days you need to sleep on a high pillow, which would reduce the swelling. Bandages are not required.

Lacrimation is possible for quite some time, especially outdoors in windy conditions. It takes a few weeks. Cosmetics can be used after 7-10 days.
