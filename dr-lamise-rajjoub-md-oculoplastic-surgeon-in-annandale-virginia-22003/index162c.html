How Long Until Plastic Surgery Stitches Dissolve?
How Long Until Plastic Surgery Stitches Dissolve?
Dec 19, 2019
This all depends on which kind of dissolvable suture was placed in the eyelids. I tend to use 6-0 fast absorbing plain gut suture which dissolves in 1-2 weeks. There are some patients who still have some residual suture at 2-3 weeks in which case it can be removed. Keep the sutures moist with antibiotic eye ointment/vaseline. They can be itchy as they are dissolving!
