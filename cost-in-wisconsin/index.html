Eye Surgery Lift Droopy Eyelids In Wisconsin

Blepharoplasty in Wisconsin is a new technique for eliminating wrinkles around the eyes, as well as treating the flabby and sagging skin of this area. The procedure is performed using a surgical diode laser. This allows to perform the cosmetic eye lift almost bloodless, with a minimal recovery period and with minimal traumatization of tissues.

When carrying out blepharoplasty with a laser, the risk of infections is significantly reduced, there is no pronounced postoperative puffiness, which provides a faster recovery than with a classical surgery with a scalpel.

Depending on the complexity of the procedure, complete rehabilitation of the patient occurs between 10 and 30 days after the surgery on eyelids.

The time for the blepharoplasty of both the upper and lower eyelids in Wisconsin is usually 0.5-3 hours and does not require general anesthesia (the exception is the desire of the patient).

Indication, time and result.

Wisconsin blepharoplasty is indicated for the correction of ptosis of eyelids and eyebrows, wrinkles around the eyes, flabbiness and sagging skin.

Laser blepharoplasty with diode surgical laser differs from the “cosmetic” CO2 laser in that the incision is present, but it is practically bloodless, only one session is needed for the procedure.

In contrast to classical blepharoplasty with a scalpel, in addition to the above, there is no need to stay in the hospital after the procedure, the postoperative period proceeds much more painlessly and quickly.

The time of the procedure is from 30 minutes to 3 hours, depending on the amount of work. The duration of the preservation of the result depends on the condition of the patient’s skin and its individual characteristics.

The skin around the eyes, continuing to recover, produces a new collagen, giving it a healthy appearance and increased elasticity.
