Miami Eye Lift Operation

What is eyelid surgery and how it is performed?

Blepharoplasty in Miami allow to get rid bags under the eyes and wrinkles. Most often the age affects the eyelids, forming bags under the eyes and wrinkles.

Usually, both of these defects exist in parallel and are removed in one operation – Eyelid surgery during which the surgeon removes and fat and excess skin. In most cases this operation is performed after the age of 30 years.

Preparation for Eyelid surgery

The patient must be in good health (no common cold or other disease during this period). It should to pass inspection (blood tests, urine tests, chest x-rays of the chest, ECG), as well as the inspection of an anesthesiologist.

If you smoke, you should stop smoking at least 2 weeks before blepharoplasty surgery and refrain from smoking after it for at least 2-3 weeks. Otherwise the risk impaired wound healing increases dramatically due to the fact that the skin blood flow in vessels is greatly reduced during smoking.

If you are taking any medication, you should discuss this with your doctor on prior consultation.

Anesthesia

Blepharoplasty is performed under anesthesia, the anesthesiologist determines option on prior consultation. It can be performed under general anesthesia or local anesthesia in combination with sedatives.

Features of the eye lift surgery procedure

Apply in basically two different types of operations: classical blepharoplasty and transconjunctival blepharoplasty in Miami.

Classical blepharoplasty

On the lower eyelid, during the operation, an incision is made along the edge of the eyelashes, which will hide the traces of surgical intervention.

The skin exfoliated to a “bag” and excess fat is removed, and the excess of exfoliated skin neatly excised. Wrinkles on the upper eyelid is removed by excision of spindle-shaped skin flap, making an incision along the length of the natural folds to hide the traces of the eye sagging surgery. The wounds are sutured with a thin surgical thread.

Upper Blepharoplasty In Miami, FL By Dr. Silvia Rotemberg

Transconjunctival blepharoplasty

This method is usually used when the lower eyelids surgery. Incision is made from the inside of the lower eyelid (conjunctiva side), i.e. without skin incisions.

The excess fat is removed. However, very often after the removal of excess fat, an excess of skin is formed, and amplified wrinkles of the lower eyelid.

In such cases, laser dermabrasion (CO2 laser) of the skin of the lower eyelids is used to an additional reduce the skin and smooth out wrinkles. Additionally, surgeon can also perform the skin resurfacing of the upper eyelids. The duration of eye surgery for droopy eyelids is 1 hour.

Postoperative period

Recovery After Surgery To Remove Excess Eyelid Skin In Florida

If you have undergone surgical correction only on the upper eyelids, the bandage not apply, and if an operation will performed on the upper and lower eyelids,gently compressive bandage (until the next morning) can prevent or reduce possible swelling. The following morning, the patient can leave the clinic.

Sutures are removed on day 3-4. The time when the patient will start to work, determined by its state of health. Usually, easy work is possible after 5-7 days. On day 11 allowed to apply light make-up, the eyes should be protected from the sun by wearing glasses with darkened glass for 6 weeks. Skin Care after laser dermabrasion is the same as after laser skin resurfacing.
