Prices Of Surgery To Lift Eyelids In Florida

Blepharoplasty, or eyelid surgery in Florida is a surgical procedure aimed at removing age-related changes in the upper and lower eyelids. Blepharoplasty of the upper and lower eyelids rightfully occupies first place among all operations in the field of anti-aging facial.

This procedure can make a woman or man younger on 7-10 years. Blepharoplasty is primarily aimed at improving and rejuvenating the upper third of the face. Operation and rehabilitation period is a short and almost painful.

Operation is easily tolerated and has a high degree of satisfaction with the result. Over time, the orbicularis oculi muscle weakens, skinfold eyelids is lowered under the influence of gravity, there are fatty hernia, having the form of lumps or protrusions in the upper and lower eyelids area.

Gradually, the wrinkles are deepening in corners of eyes. Given that the eyes are the “calling card” of our face, it is not surprising that women give special attention to this area.

Indications for this operation are determined easily: Stand in front of a mirror and examine your eyes carefully. It is easily to determine the excess folds of skin in the upper eyelid area, taking it with two fingers.

Then close one eye and gently press it – look as far fat protrusions are visible in the upper and lower eyelids a closed eyes. Essence of the operation involves the removal of excess skin and overhang, and also the removal of fat protrusion.

When properly performed blepharoplasty, eyes become more open, the overall appearance is more fresh and rested.

4 Lid Blepharoplasty With Dr Roberto E. Garcia

The methods and a technique of the operation.

Upper Blepharoplasty in Florida is performed under local anesthesia. The mean duration of surgery is 30-40min. During the operation, excess skin is excised, which aggravates the upper eyelid.

Also, fat hernia is removed which protrude in the upper eyelid area and cause swelling in this area. Postoperative seam is a thin and accurate, later 2-4 weeks becomes invisible.

Florida Lower blepharoplasty is performed under general anesthesia, sometimes under local anesthesia. The mean duration of surgery is 30-40min. Lower blepharoplasty can be traditional or transconjunctival.

The incision in the traditional lower blepharoplasty is performed under the lash line of the lower eyelid, along it. Through this access swelling of the lower eyelid and excess skin are removed. During transconjunctival blepharoplasty removed only fat protrusion (swelling) and performed for young people, who do not have excess skin, and have bags under eyes.

Postoperative period – recovery.

You’ll be in the clinic one day after such an operation, and then you able go home. Postoperative suture is removed 3-4 days. After a few days you will be able to apply makeup.

Bruising and swelling going down after 10-14days. Cosmetic procedures to eliminate the swelling and bruises are appointed 4-5 days after surgery. They will help speed up the recovery process after blepharoplasty. You can return to normal physical activities and go to work 12-14 days. The final result is evaluated through 1-1,5 months. The effect of this operation is retained 7-10 years.

Dark circles under the eyes, the apparent excess skin in the lower eyelids, deep nasolacrimal furrow, bags (swelling of the cheeks, under the lower eyelids), wrinkles in the “crow’s feet” area, glabellar lines can not be eliminated with the help of blepharoplasty.

6 Months After Double Eyelid Surgery With Dr John J. Martin

Wrinkles in this area is eliminated with Botox and hyaluronic acid preparations. This procedure can be performed 2-3 weeks after blepharoplasty.

Eyelid surgery is indicated in the following cases:

  1. the presence of fatty deposits, hernias, which creating the effect of “bags” under the eyes;
  2. overhang of the upper eyelid due to an excess of skin;
  3. midface ptosis;
  4. the desire to change the shape of the eye;
  5. puffiness “heavy, frowning” view does not correspond to your inner state.

Advantages of blepharoplasty in FL

It’s getting the maximum aesthetic effect. Eyelid surgery can eliminate excess skin, the overhang, hernia under the eyes and excess fat, forming a “bags” under the eyes. A look becomes open, as it was in the younger years. If necessary, blepharoplasty can be supplemented with other rejuvenation techniques, such as facelift.

Blepharoplasty 10 Days After By Dr Ross A. Clevens

Surgical intervention is minimal. During the intervention, the surgeon makes small incisions in the natural folds of the skin. Furthermore, special cosmetic sutures are used that does not remain scars after operation.

Transconjunctival blepharoplasty in Florida is performed through a tiny incision on the inside of the eyelid. This type of blepharoplasty is suitable for patients with fatty hernias, but with enough elastic skin around the eyelids without excess skin.

The described method also will greatly improve the appearance of those who are far from aging – young men and women who want to get rid of the shortcomings in the eye area.

The recovery period is short. The operation does not require hospitalization and discontinuation of active lifestyle for the long term. After blepharoplasty, seams pasted over with strips of special patch, they will be removed a day later, and the small bruises disappear after a few days.
