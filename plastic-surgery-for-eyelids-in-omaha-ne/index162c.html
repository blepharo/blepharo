Upper Eyelid Blepharoplasty.

The purpose of the upper blepharoplasty is to return the natural look of your eyelids:

– To remove excess skin, which was formed on the eyelids due to loss of elasticity;

– relieve you of protruding fat deposits on the upper eyelid, which enhance the morning puffiness and unattractiveness;

What type of anesthesia is used during surgery blepharoplasty?

When the upper eyelid surgery is used more often a local anesthesia, but also possible, and general anesthesia.

What are the stages of the upper eyelids surgery?

First need to determine the the excess skin that must be excised, and the presence or absence of fat (the so-called fat packages).

The incision lines are drawn in a sitting position, the upper eyelid skin is captured a pinch with your fingers or with tweezers and excess tissue is marked up for excision.

Immediately after the eye surgery will be applied a special bandage and cold. Within 1-2 hours, you will be with your eyes closed. Lower blepharoplasty.

Lower blepharoplasty in Omaha is performed to eliminate the “bags under the eyes,” the removal of excess skin from the lower eyelid, to eliminating grooves under the eyelid. In some case it is necessary to lift up the omitted corner of the eye.

Omaha, Nebraska Blepharoplasty For Droopy Upper Eyelids And Lower Lid Bags

Depending on the incision, the lower eyelid surgery is divided into classic – an incision is made on the skin under the eyelashes and transconjunctival – an incision is made inside the eye (in the conjunctiva). The skin incision is made receding from lash edge of 3 mm along the lower lid and parallel to it. Only the outer incision line goes beyond the border of 5-6 mm.

The skin is separated from the circular muscle of the eye. The muscle is crossed along the section line. The fat herniation (fat packages) are removed.

A special supporting internal seams are applied, which provide a stable result blepharoplasty. The excess lower eyelid skin is removed. Sutures are applied on the muscle and skin.

Recovery after lower blepharoplasty.

Lower eyelid surgery in Omaha is a more complex operation than the upper blepharoplasty so recovery times is longer. Just after surgery on eyelids the eye remain closed under the pressure bandage from a few hours to half a day.

When a very complex blepharoplasty, eye at the outer edge of the are stitched (upper and lower eyelid together). At the same time person sees, but can not completely open his eyes. This approach prevents the development of severe edema and possibly, inversion eyelid.
