Laser vs. cautery vs. scalpel for blepharoplasty

There have been many articles published on which is superior for blepharoplasty in Syracuse, and most have shown no difference at 2-3 weeks post-op.

Flying is a bad idea

  1. If you sustain a bleed behind your eye, which the risk is highest in the first 24-48 hours after bottom eyelid surgery, you will be in flight on a plane. There is a risk with a bleed of losing permanent vision. It is a huge risk to take.

  3. If a stitch breaks you will be midflight and then what are you going to do? catch a flight immediately back when you get home? Call around for a surgeon to fix the broken stitch All in all, you should be around for 24 hours minimum after eyelid surgery
