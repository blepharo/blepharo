Do not trust a blepharoplasty for surgeon who says … Only about their achievements, you should be wary. Achievements is a right thing: a good plastic surgeon should be experienced in the first place. “remove swelling” is better to say farewell with it.

Swelling under the eyes is the result of heredity, internal diseases or lifestyle. Scalpel is unable to cope with it. It can only remove excess skin, which is stretched due to edema.

If you can not eliminate internal problems, does not to stop eating salty and abundant drink before going to bed – puffiness and, as a result, sagging skin again make themselves felt. Connecticut Blepharoplasty can be entrusted on the surgeon who …

Doctor will explain, that blepharoplasty does not relieve you from the crow’s feet and fine wrinkles on the eyelids: as a result of the operation, you can say goodbye only with bags under the eyes, the overhang upper eyelid, subcutaneous adipose hernias.

He’ll tell you that in most cases, after the circular blepharoplasty (when the surgeon works with the upper and the lower eyelids) eyes becomes somewhat rounder in shape.

Of course, you should discuss the possible risks the eyelid surgery with your doctor, such as:

  1. dry eye, or, conversely, lacrimation (these symptoms often occur after surgery, but successfully disappear);
  2. eversion of the lower eyelid, after which a face acquires bulldog features, this trouble can occur with poor muscle tone or if a lot of skin has been removed;
  3. inability to close the eye completely after upper blepharoplasty.

A good doctor will not dance to your tune, and to perform all your desires. For example, blepharoplasty is recommended to do no more frequently than once every 7-10 years.

If you come before, the doctor, at least, must warn that the responsibility for the result, and will lay on you also, in an ideal – to protect you from ill-considered step.

How is a blepharoplasty performed?

The operation is performed under general or local anesthesia, with minimal preparations for blepharoplasty: a traditional set of analyzes, consultation an anesthesiologist, to stop taking drugs that violate the blood clotting (include aspirin).

Tobacco intoxication violates the blood circulation in the small vessels, including vessels the skin eyelids, so for 2-3 weeks prior to surgery is desirable to abandon smoking.

When the upper blepharoplasty in Connecticut, an incision is made along the natural folds of the eyelids – a cosmetic seam at this place is imperceptible.

When the lower eyelid blepharoplasty, the incision is performed under the lashes. If there is no excess sagging skin, and only need to remove the fat herniation (such operations often performed to 30 years old, when the skin is still in good shape), it is possible to perform transconjunctival blepharoplasty: incision is made from the inside of the eyelid, the seam is not required at all – mucous membranes are regenerated much faster skin.

Doctor Leon Goldstein Surgical Repair Of The Eyelid Result Image

During transconjunctival lower eyelid surgery in Connecticut often use a laser instead of a scalpel. It is used to remove wrinkles around the eyes.

In most cases, eyelid surgery does not require hospitalization: within 12 hours after the surgery you can to go home. Sutures are removed after 2-3 days. The first 1-1,5 months is better to abstain from severe physical exertion.

Yes, the recovery after blepharoplasty lasts 1.5 months: the bruises go through 10-14 days, and then gradually and swelling, but the effect is worth it.

The ideal result of blepharoplasty is not a change in eye shape, and only correction of age changes.

How to save the result of eyelid surgery (blepharoplasty) for a long time?

As before surgery and after, your age will be reflected first of all in your eyes, and hence, on the skin around the eyes. The whole point of anatomy: the eyelids is the thinnest layer of the epidermis and without sebaceous glands, weak muscles (hence the fatty tissue forms a hernia), the lower eyelids are often prone to edema, plus to this in the form of wrinkles on the eyelids.

Dr. Ellen A. Mahony Plastic Surgery Eye Lift For Eyes With Drooping Upper Eyelids Or Puffy Bags Below The Eyes

Therefore, prevention of the need to re blepharoplasty includes the same items as the skin care around the eyes:

  1. Healthy sleep on the pillow properly selected;
  2. The use of special creams and serums for the skin around the eyes, providing hydration, nutrition, reduces swelling;
  3. Lymphatic drainage massage of the face (particularly useful in the postoperative period and regularly for those who are prone to edema);
  4. Consultation of competent beautician, who correctly will organize anti-aging treatments in general and in particular the care of the skin around the eyes.

Contraindications for eyelid surgery (blepharoplasty) in Connecticut.

Contraindications are the same as for any other operation: cardiovascular and oncological diseases, high blood pressure, blood clotting problems, diabetes, any chronic diseases in the acute stage.
