Eyelid surgery: Preparation for surgery.

Before After Blepharoplasty In Phoenix, Arizona Image

In most cases, some sort of special preparation for eyelid lifting is not required. The emotional mood of the patient largely depends on him, as well as the level of trust to the plastic surgeon.

Medicinal preparations, eating, smoking and other issues are discussed with your plastic surgeon. Please, follow recommendations of your surgeon, is too much attention to the opinions of other people can degrade the result of the plastic surgery for eyelids , and your condition as a whole.

If you smoke, it is desirable to reduce the number of cigarettes or completely stop smoking for 1 – 2 weeks before and after blepharoplasty.

As a result of smoking decreases blood flow to the skin, as a result post-operative wounds heal slower than a nonsmoking patients.

During the 10 days prior to the blepharoplasty in Phoenix is recommended to stop taking alcohol. During the 10 days prior to blepharoplasty, you should stop taking aspirin and other medicaments affecting blood clotting.

Recommendations for women:

Contraceptives or other hormonal drugs can cause bleeding. This issue is solved on an individual basis at the consultation with a plastic surgeon.

Most often, is recommended to stop taking this kind of drug for 10-14 days prior to blepharoplasty.

The corrective eyelid surgery can not be done during the period of menstruation, so you should plan the date of the surgery for eyelids in such a way that it does not hit the menstrual period (menstruation days should be excluded, as well as 3-5 days before and after). Eyelid surgery often takes place in an outpatient basis and the patient can leave the clinic after 1 -2 hours after surgery.

At the request of the patient, he may remain in the hospital for a day. If Blepharoplasty is performed in conjunction with a face lift, the patient’s hospital stay increased to 2-3 days.

Blepharoplasty.

Duration of blepharoplasty depends on its volume and generally takes 1-3 hours. If there are indications, to the patient is performed the upper and / or lower eyelid surgery. The plastic surgeon makes marking on eyelids of the patient immediately before bottom eyelid surgery.

Lower eyelids surgery.

Phoenix blepharoplasty of lower eyelids is performed in two ways – classic and transconjunctival. Transconjunctival eyelid surgery is performed for a fairly youthful patients in the presence of bags under the eyes and in the absence of excess skin. In these cases, excess fat is removed through an incision from the conjunctiva, blepharoplasty is performed without visible external incision.

If the patient has obvious excess skin of the lower eyelids – then is performed a classic lower blepharoplasty. The incision is made at a distance of 2-3 mm from the lash line of the lower eyelids. The last step of the classical blepharoplasty is suturing. A few days after the blepharoplasty eyelid surgery the sutures are removed. Skin age is very thin and so the scars in this area heal quickly enough. Scar surprisingly quickly takes the form of a thin white barely visible thread.

Upper eyelids blepharoplasty.

Blepharoplasty of upper eyelids in Phoenix performs in the case of excess skin and hernias. Most often, upper eyelid blepharoplasty performed for cosmetic purpose, but there are also cases where excess skin is so large that hang on the eyes and prevent a person normally see. The upper blepharoplasty is always carried out with external incisions.

This is only way to remove the excess skin and excess fat. Before the eye lift operation, the plastic surgeon does markings on the upper eyelids. The length the incision for each patient individually and depends on the excess skin of the upper eyelids. According to indications, in some cases, is performed partially excision of the muscle (without this manipulation the aesthetic result of blepharoplasty can be quite weakly expressed).
