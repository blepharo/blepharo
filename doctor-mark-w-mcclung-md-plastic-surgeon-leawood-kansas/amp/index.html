Should canthopexy always be performed with lower bleph (fat repositioning) to maintain eyelid shape
Is Canthopexy mandatory
Apr 3, 2020
The short answer is no. The need for canthopexy is dependent on technique and the patients anatomy. In most cases where I do a trasconjunctival approach, it is because there is little overall lid laxity and there will be minimal skin excision. In more aggressive cases with muscle division and /or release of insertions at the orbital rim, and/or existing lid laxity then a canthopexy is appropriate
