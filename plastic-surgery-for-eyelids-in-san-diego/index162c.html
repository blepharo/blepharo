Plastic Surgery For Eyelids In San Diego CA

Blepharoplasty, or eyelid surgery In San Diego is the operative intervention aimed at changing the shape of the eyelids or eye shape.

This is surgery to remove excess skin of the lower or upper eyelid, for eliminating the wrinkled folds of skin, fat hernias, removal of puffiness and bags under the eyes.

The technique involves the excision of surgically excess fat layer, the tension of the skin.

Blepharoplasty is performed under local anesthesia by making incisions in the recess of the upper eyelid or lash line the lower eyelid.

The duration of eyelid correction surgery is between two and five hours. The recovery period takes about 10-12 days.

There are indications for blepharoplasty: overhanging skin of the upper or lower eyelid, “fat bags” on the eyelids, omitted lower corners of the eyes and shape of the eyes.

Blepharoplasty does not eliminate the dark circles under the eyes, small mimic wrinkles and “crow’s feet”. The operation is not possible without a comprehensive consultation with a specialist.

There are the following types of blepharoplasty:

1. Upper eyelid blepharoplasty San Diego CA. It eliminates the overhang and is conducted mostly through the skin incision, which is located in the folds of the upper eyelids. This incision eliminates the excess skin. Upper eyelid blepharoplasty is carried out in conjunction with the plastic upper eyelid muscles and removal of excess fatty tissue, although sometimes it is enough just to remove the skin.

2. The lower eyelid blepharoplasty. It removes bags under the eyes, swelling and herniation. The seam remain on the edge lash line. Transconjunctival blepharoplasty is also performed on the lower eyelid, where the incision is made on an internal side of the eyelids.

This operation is possible only if it is necessary to redistribute or remove the fatty tissue around the eyes without removing excess skin. This type of operation is relevant for young patients.

3. Circular blepharoplasty is the simultaneous operation of the upper and lower eyelids.

4. Asian Eyelid Surgery.

Peculiarities of the upper eyelids surgery

Upper Eyelid Blepharoplasty Post Op By Dr Amir M. Karam, M.D

This plastic surgery performed under local anesthesia, and under intravenous anesthesia. The operation takes from one hour to one and a half hours.

The first few hours after surgery, ice is applied on the intervention site, allowing to reduce the sizes of postoperative swelling. After the expiry of a local anesthetic, the patient may feel а slightly pulsatile bruise around the eye.

However, patients rarely feel a lot of pain. Discomfort are removed with a mild analgesic. Painkillers are not necessary for most patients. As a rule, patients can leave the hospital the day of surgery.

Postoperative bruises begin to pale and turn yellow after 2-3 days, and almost completely disappear in 10-12 days. Sutures are removed on the 4th day after the surgery and a week later they are almost invisible.

Lower blepharoplasty

The lower eyelid is performed only in a hospital and most often under general anesthesia. The operation takes from half an hour to forty minutes. As in the upper blepharoplasty, cosmetic sutures are superimposed on the incision site.

After completing the operation the bandage is applied, which should be changed to the next day after eye tuck surgery, and can only be removed after 3-4 days when the sutures are removed. The patient will discharged immediately after the operation or after changing the bandage on the next day.

The first three days after surgery doctors often advised to use eye drops. Already on the second day after surgery on the lower eyelids may need special exercises for the eyes. The patient will be able to evaluate the final result of lower blepharoplasty not earlier than six weeks.

How is a circular blepharoplasty performed?

Eye Cosmetic Procedures California Pic Before And After

Before the circular blepharoplasty not only the surgeon examines the patient, but also narrow specialists such as an ophthalmologist. It is explained by a serious interference in the area of of ocular fiber, which occurs during the operation. Blepharoplasty on the upper and lower eyelids is performed under general anesthesia in conjunction with the local anesthesia in the eye area.

During circular blepharoplasty surgeon cuts the upper eyelid along the natural crease, removes excess skin and fat, or tightens the orbicularis oculi muscle. During the same operation and lower eyelids is cut, and surgeon is perform the necessary lifting of the skin.

Fatty tissue above and below the eyes is often not removed and can be redistributed. This technique allows to remove bags under the eyes, but also to prevent the occurrence of pits in the skin after eyelid sagging surgery.

Often during the circular blepharoplasty is also carried out the eye canthopexy and canthoplasty.

The patient is under medical supervision for a day after the upper and lower eyelids surgery. Immediately after the operation on his eyes tight bandage is applied, which is removed after 10-12 hours.

Asian eyelid surgery

This type is considered the most difficult kind of operation. Asian population has several significant differences from European population: no wrinkles on the upper eyelid; inexpressive inner corner of eye, which almost entirely covers epicanthus (skin fold); quite a lot of fatty tissue around the eyes.

The goal Asian blepharoplasty in San Diego CA is to give the similarity with European shaped eyes, with the maximum possible. During this plastic surgery for eyes are formed folds of the upper eyelids, correction of epicanthus and remove excess fatty tissue. The upper eyelid is cut exactly along the line, where will be located formed crease.

A thin strip of skin and excess fatty tissue is removed. Cosmetic seam is applied to the incision, which after recovery is almost invisible. Epicanthus eliminate mandatory. If necessary, fat hernia under eyes removed through an incision. The eyelid drooping surgery is performed under general anesthesia. The patient must be at the hospital one day after Asian eyelid surgery.
