When I should have to resort to surgery blepharoplasty? Some people have to resort to blepharoplasty at the age of 30, and some do not need eyelid surgery and 45 years old.

It all depends on the characteristics of the eye structure. The eyelids (at a depth of 3-5 mm) have a special membrane that serves as a sort of barrier that separates “intraocular” fat from the skin.

Alas, with age this membrane becomes thinner and appears the gap, and sebum comes out, forming bags in the eyelid area. As a result, the thin skin of the eyelids start sagging, rough wrinkles are formed, which can be eliminate with a blepharoplasty in Honolulu.

Depending on the severity of the changes and the reasons that provoke their occurrence is depend the amount, technique, price of blepharoplasty, and, importantly, the degree of satisfaction with the result of the eye tuck surgery and duration of the effect of blepharoplasty! Therefore, prior to blepharoplasty is necessary to pass a comprehensive examination of the body.

Blepharoplasty is a delicate matter!

And before starting the eyelids surgery is necessary to determine degree of expression age-related facial changes.

1st degree: slight changes in the lower eyelids, a slight excess of skin on the lower and upper eyelid; this includes the so-called hernia eyelids, or bags in which adipose tissue pulls the eyelids.

The solution is to “upper and lower blepharoplasty with removal of a small amount of fat and skin” or alternative procedure “redistribution” intraocular fat.

2nd degree: more pronounced changes, not only in eyelids area, but also the cheekbones (subcutaneous fat goes down, sharply delineating the cheekbones), and the boundary between the eyelid and cheek becomes too noticeable. The solution is to filling of the spaces with the intraorbital fat with the help of blepharoplasty.

3rd degree: in addition to worsening the boundaries between the eyelid and cheek, tear trough (from the inner corner of the eye and down to the nose), there is a significant ptosis of the midface and clearly-pronounced nasolabial folds. Decision. Operation blepharoplasty is complemented by simultaneous facelifting, otherwise the effect of blepharoplasty quickly come to naught! Another option for upper eyelids is a combinations blepharoplasty with a forehead lift (weakening of the muscles may be the cause lowering the eyebrows and formation of the bag in the area of the upper eyelid).

4th degree: clearly visible tear troughs, your eyes rounded, bags are placed not just below the eyes, and below, in the cheekbone area. The solution is a global surgery so-called “median lift” of the face, in which not only the skin is lifting and the bags are removed with the help of blepharoplasty and several is redistributed tension ligaments that hold the muscles.

Contraindications to blepharoplasty:

  • Chronic heart or respiratory failure;
  • Conditions after heart attack and stroke;
  • Complications of diabetes;
  • blood diseases;
  • Some systemic diseases (eg, lupus erythematosus);
  • Acute viral infection or exacerbation of chronic diseases.

Recovery after blepharoplasty

The sutures are removed 2-3 days after blepharoplasty, and after 10-14 days the swelling and bruises goes away. In the postoperative period are advised to wear sunglasses: they will save from the bright sunlight and hide from prying eyes. You can use cosmetics 2 weeks after blepharoplasty in Honolulu.

Potential complications after blepharoplasty:

  • constant lacrimation;
  • inability to close the eye completely;
  • excessive omitted of the outer corner of the eye;
  • dry eyes;
  • conjunctivitis;
  • blurred vision and bleeding (very rarely).
