Eyelid Surgery In New York City

Blepharoplasty (eyelid surgery) in NYC is plastic surgery on the upper (or) lower eyelids in which course the appearance of a century improves, and eliminates or reduces aging changes around the eyes.

Blepharoplasty can be performed as an isolated procedure or in combination with other aesthetic operations. Eyelid surgery allows to remove bags under the eyes, folds and wrinkles around the eyes.

The eyes are the aesthetic center of the face, so blepharoplasty is used, for improving the appearance of the whole, and to eliminate the signs of aging in this area. Blepharoplasty gives the face a more youthful, energetic and rested appearance.

Preparing for blepharoplasty

Blepharoplasty in New York City involves quite a serious intervention in the field of orbital fat. Therefore, you must pass a full medical examination before blepharoplasty.

Eyelid Surgery: the operation.

Blepharoplasty performed in the stationary conditions under local anesthesia, or anesthesia in combination with a local anesthetic. The operation can take from 30 minutes to 2 hours. There are many techniques of blepharoplasty, some of them are outdated, some are used to this day. Modern techniques of blepharoplasty involve almost imperceptible incisions century, which are located along the natural folds. Among them there are methods allowing to perform blepharoplasty without external incisions and without stitches. The dressing is superimposed on the wound after the operation, which is removed after 4-6 hours after surgery. Pain in the postoperative period are not expressed.

The recovery time for eyelid surgery

5 Months Post Blepharoplasty In NY

Suture removal is carried out 4-5 days after blepharoplasty in NYC. As a rule, you may be discharged from the hospital in the evening after eye surgery or the next day.

Within 1-2 weeks after blepharoplasty may experience swelling of the tissues. Bruises usually disappear after 5-8 days after baggy eyelid surgery. Most patients can return to work after 7-10 days after the eyelid surgery. Since that time, you can use hypoallergenic cosmetics.

Sunglasses not only perfectly protect from ultraviolet radiation, but also from prying eyes, so it is recommended to use them for 1-2 weeks after blepharoplasty. In most cases, scarring is not visible after blepharoplasty, but the effect remains forever.

Blepharoplasty in New York City is also contraindicated in chronic diseases of the eyelids and conjunctiva. The result of the eyelid surgery is more open and lively sight, combined with a smaller skin overhang. Operation technique in each case selected individually.

The duration of the operation depends on the volume and complexity of the necessary procedures. Usually surgical intervention takes from 30 minutes to an hour. Most often, blepharoplasty is done under local anesthesia. At the same time the eyelids and the area around them anesthetized, and the patient is in a relaxed state.
