Can a deep plane lift be performed on somebody who has silicone cheek implants?
Deep Plane Facelift After Cheek Implants
Jun 15, 2020
Yes, you can have a Deep Plane Facelift after silicone cheek implants.  Your cheek implants are placed just above your cheek bones in a deeper plane than even a "deep plane" facelift.  When performed properly, a Deep Plane Facelift should not disturb your implants and complement the look you have achieved from cheek implants.

Asymmetry following Eyelid Surgery
Aug 2, 2018
Asymmetry following eyelid surgery can be due to many reasons.  Pre-existing asymmetry, eyelid ptosis or drooping, brow asymmetries and swelling are just some of the reasons.  You should allow some more time for swelling to resolve and then compare your before and after photos with your surgeon to determine the cause.   Unfortunately there are no specific exercises that will help but massaging your scar will certainly not hurt the situation.
