Plastic Surgery For Eyelids In The State Of Washington

Blepharoplasty (eyelid surgery) in Washington is performed by special fractional laser. The operation will help get rid of excess sagging skin under the eyes, wrinkles around the eyes (deep and “crow’s feet”), restore muscle tone.

One of the most popular operations among plastic surgery of the face is a blepharoplasty. This is understandable, because the skin around the eyes is very delicate and one of the first subjected to the aging process.

Furthermore, primarily attracting attention. Eyelid surgery is to return the young and attractive look, and every woman regain confidence in their uniqueness.

The difference between the surgical and laser method.

For a long time, blepharoplasty in Washington was performed surgically, using a conventional scalpel, reaching thus a good result. But the difficulty is that after the baggy eye surgery there were bruises, swelling, pain due to trauma.

Recovery can last up to 3-4 weeks. In addition, it is impossible to remove “crow’s feet” wrinkles and using classical surgery. Also among the complications that are possible with the standard surgical technique of blepharoplasty should be noted the visibility postoperative scar, contracture or the possibility of inversion of the eyelid.

These complications is devoid the technique of laser eyelid surgery. It is therefore particularly popular at the moment acquired a method such as laser blepharoplasty in Washington. Laser methods are used for successful operation on the eyelids. They are distinguished by their precision and because of them achieved more pronounced results.

Moreover, the recovery period is significantly reduced. The time for recovery from the eye operation is only 3 days. Makeup can be applied on the second day after surgery, and on the skin around the eyes does not remains even traces after complete recovery!

Signs of aging eyes.

The main symptoms of aging eyes include:

  • the appearance of “crow’s feet” or wrinkles;
  • the appearance of facial wrinkles;
  • deepening nasolacrimal furrow;
  • sagging of excess skin;
  • the appearance of fat herniation (bags under the eyes);
  • the appearance of deep wrinkles around the eyes;
  • loss of muscle tone, surrounding the eye.

Eyelid surgery – features of carrying out of operation.

Laser blepharoplasty is performed with transconjunctival method, ie internal access through the mucosa of the eyelids. As a result of this are removed (or evaporate) fat herniation (bags under the eyes), stimulated the reduction and restructuring of the skin, restores muscle tone.

This result is achieved by applying a special fractional ablative laser (CO2 Smart Xide DOT), thanks to which restored the skin’s surface layer. The method is used more than 10 years in Europe and gaining popularity.
