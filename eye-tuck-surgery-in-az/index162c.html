What is a blepharoplasty?

Blepharoplasty (eyelid surgery) In Arizona is a surgical correction of the upper and lower eyelid for the purpose of aesthetic rejuvenation. This operation allows to remove fat hernia with upper eyelids, change eye shape and save the patient from the bags under the eyes (if they are provoked hernias).

Blepharoplasty is not able to cope with such aesthetic problems such as wrinkles in corners of eyes. This requires the implementation of other operations and procedures, for example, resurfacing or using injection techniques.

Would I be a good candidate for Blepharoplasty?

Most often, blepharoplasty in AZ prescribed to people of mature age (40 and older) which have wrinkles on the upper eyelid, a hernia or excess skin, as a result it falls on the eyelashes, making the person visually older.

Protrusion of infraorbital fat (the so-called bags under the eyes) may also be an indication for blepharoplasty. This operation solves other problems, such as the omitted corners of the eyes (congenital or age).

How is cosmetic surgery on eyes performed?

Chandler Arizona Lower Blepharoplasty Can Give People A Refreshed, Alert Appearance

If Blepharoplasty in AZ is performed without the circular face lift or other rejuvenation operations, it may carry out under local anesthesia.

The surgeon makes incisions of the upper or lower eyelids, and depending on the problem removes excess skin, reduces a fat hernia or removes fatty tissue.

After that, cosmetic sutures are superimposed, which are practically not visible to others people, as the hidden under natural skin folds.

The blepharoplasty for bags under eyes lasts an average of 20 minutes to 1.5 hours, depending on the amount of work.

How long is the postoperative period?

7 Days After Upper Eyelid Surgery In Lake Havasu City AZ

Immediately after eyelid drooping surgery, tight bandage is superimposed to the eyes of the patient, which is removed after 4-5 hours. Sutures are removed after 5-7 days, during this time, as a rule, postoperative swelling and bruises are disappearing. Bruising may persists 10-30 days.

But this does not mean that the patient has to stay at home for a month after blepharoplasty. You can leave the house immediately after the removal of sutures. However, you should protect the eyes with sunglasses and hypoallergenic cosmetics.

How long does it take effect?

The rejuvenating effect of blepharoplasty usually lasts 5-7 years. Repeated operations on the lower and upper eyelid can be performed no more than 3 times, although in some cases, exceptions are made.

Complications.

AZ Eyelid Surgery To Remove Lump Before And After Photos

The cosmetic eye lift is a low-traumatic. Entering of an infection or damage to the cornea of the eye is minimal. The only thing that may not like the patient is a slight change in shape of the eye (they can become rounder, corners of the eyes and lower eyelids may slightly goes down). Therefore, the task of a surgeon to warn the patient about all possible risks before eye lid plastic surgery.

Contraindications:

  1. Diseases of the thymus gland, during which decreases the tone of muscles;
  2. Diseases of the skin around the eyes;
  3. Numerous operations to blepharoplasty (the presence of large scars under the skin);
  4. Injections of fillers in the eye area (proteins, silicone);
  5. diabetes (very badly healed wound edge).
