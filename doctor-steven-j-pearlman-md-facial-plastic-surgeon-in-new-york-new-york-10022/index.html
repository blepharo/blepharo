Do Nose Reshaping Devices Work? I Am Trying to Correct an Asymmetrical Nose Tip.
Nose shaping devices do not work
Mar 2, 2020
Cartilage is resilient and will go back to it's original form right after taking any devices off. The only cartilage shaping device that works is when a baby is born with a deformed ear. Placing a device on the ear for a few months can create a more normal shape. This is when the cartialge is still forming. After babies are 6 months old, even these devices do not work.
