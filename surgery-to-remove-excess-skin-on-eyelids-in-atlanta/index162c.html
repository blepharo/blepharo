Eyelid surgery or blepharoplasty In Atlanta GA is a surgical correction, which allows to remove “bags” under the eyes, wrinkles, folds of skin, and perform the shape eye correction on request of the patient.

The eyes is the aesthetic center of the face. It is therefore, eyelid surgery is not only improves the appearance, in general, but also eliminates the signs of aging in the face area.

Eyelid surgery makes the face younger and energetic. Eyelid surgery can be performed as a separate procedure, and can be included in a complex of cosmetic surgeries.

Age and indications. Blepharoplasty is shown, in most cases, with the obvious manifestation of age-related changes. Bags under the eyes, and the severity and the overhang of the upper eyelids, sullen eyes, swelling of the morning, unfortunately, are not eliminated by the use of therapeutic techniques. Eyelid surgery, at the moment, is one of the most popular plastic surgeries because negative age-related changes, very often occur after 30 years old.

How to prepare for the surgical reduction of the eyelids. Patients, who are planning to blepharoplasty, you must undergo a full clinical examination, including consultation ophthalmologist, as contraindications for surgery, can be exclusively on that line.

It should be noted that wearing contact lenses is excluded after blepharoplasty. Therefore, the patient need to pre-purchase the appropriate glasses.

Operation. Duration blepharoplasty upper and lower eyelids is about 2 hours, when performed simultaneously, and about 1 hour if only upper blepharoplasty is performed or only the lower eyelid surgery.
