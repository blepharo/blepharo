Houston Eyelid Cosmetic Surgery

Blepharoplasty in Houston is the functional and aesthetic surgery for the correction of the upper or lower eyelids (including simultaneous circular blepharoplasty) in order to remove excess stretched skin and strengthen circular muscles of the eye. In some cases, a significant stretching of the skin can be observed with its excess.

The skin may hang over the eyelashes and lead to a decrease the peripheral vision. The significant narrowing of the field of view causes a difficulty with reading and driving.

In this case, the upper blepharoplasty is performed to improve peripheral vision. Patients who have less pronounced excess skin, such blepharoplasty is performed for aesthetic reasons.

Lower eyelid blepharoplasty surgery is usually performed with a cosmetic purpose to remove hernia “bags” under the eyes, and excess skin.

Classical blepharoplasty of the upper eyelids is performed through an external incision along the natural lines, namely in the crease of the upper eyelid.

The skin incision at the lower classical blepharoplasty passes through the outer edge of the lower eyelid, retreating 1 mm below lash line, making the seam completely invisible. When transconjunctival blepharoplasty,the incision passes through the inside of the mucosa.

Postoperative hematoma and swelling resolves within 1-2 weeks, but the final aesthetic result of blepharoplasty can be estimated no sooner than a month. The duration of the operation depends on the technology and the complexity of the case. Typically, this operation lasts for 1-2 hours.

Features of the structure of the patient’s skin, his anatomical differences, age, individual structure of the bones of the orbit, in which located the eyeball and surrounding tissue are affecting the aesthetic and functional results after blepharoplasty.

Factors that may cause complications after surgical blepharoplasty:

  1. Preoperative dryness can cause damage to the mucous membranes of the eyes;
  2. Excessive pulling (low tone) the edge of the lower eyelid can lead to the wrong contour, lower than the natural position of its external part (ectropion);
  3. Bulging eyes relative to the cheeks can become the reason an incorrect position of the lower eyelid after blepharoplasty.

The average cost of blepharoplasty surgery in Houston TX is about $ 3000 US dollars. The reputation of the clinic and materials used for the surgery affects on the formation of the price.

It should be remembered that in many clinics in the price of blepharoplasty not include the overhead of anesthesia, dressings, hospitalization, cost of preoperative tests package.

The aesthetic results of blepharoplasty can be estimated using the photo gallery of patients before and after surgery.

Houston upper blepharoplasty, which is carried to natives of Asia called Asian eyelid surgery. This operation is the most popular in aesthetic surgery among residents of East and Southeast Asia. Asian eyelid surgery is performed to create an artificial upper eyelid crease.

Transconjunctival blepharoplasty is performed to remove the adipose tissue in the lower eyelid area through the incision along the internal surface (through a mucous membrane) without the outer skin incision.

That is why the transconjunctival blepharoplasty is not designed to remove excess skin. However, this technique is often combined with a chemical peel or laser skin rejuvenation periorbital area.

In the center of plastic surgery in Houston are performed various operations for the rejuvenation of the periorbital region. Among the various surgical techniques are used blepharoplasty upper eyelid with invisible seams, Asian eyelid surgery, during which a natural fold is formed, lower eyelid blepharoplasty, including minimally invasive transconjunctival blepharoplasty.
