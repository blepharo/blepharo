What are the best candidates for blepharoplasty?

Indiana blepharoplasty, like many other plastic surgery, should be performing for healthy patients, in the absence of contraindications to eyelid procedure.

Plastic surgeries, including and blepharoplasty are undesirable for patients with chronic diseases (diabetes, hypertension, heart diseases), as they have a higher risk of complications during and after cosmetic surgery eye lift, as well as wound healing is worse.

In some cases, blepharoplasty can not provide the desired effect, since the problem may affect not only the eyelids, but also other areas of the face, in particular the skin of the forehead and eyebrows.

Blepharoplasty of lower eyelids in Indiana is recommended to perform if presence the swollen bags under the eyes. Usually, bottom eyelid surgery is performed under general anesthesia. The incisions are made along the natural creases.
