Jun 29, 2019
The goal of microneedling with PRP treatments is to stimulate collagen growth on the skin in order to improve its overall quality and texture.  It does not make your face plump the way fillers do. Some patients elect to have PRFM (platelet rich fibrin matrix - a more solidified form of PRP) to be injected at the same time and that can be used for volume augmentation.  Goretti H. Taghva MD FACS
