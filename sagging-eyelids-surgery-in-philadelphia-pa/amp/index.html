Sagging Eyelids Surgery In Philadelphia

Blepharoplasty in Philadelphia is a surgical method for the correction of the upper and lower eyelids.

The goal of blepharoplasty is to remove the overhanging skin folds on the upper eyelid with a correction of hernias, as well as the removal of excess skin on the lower eyelid with correction of bags under the eyes.

Currently, Eyelid surgery is one of the most popular plastic surgery operations in the world.

There are several types of eyelid surgery (blepharoplasty) in Philadelphia PA

– Upper blepharoplasty
– Lower blepharoplasty
– Lower transconjunctival blepharoplasty.

Are You a Good Candidate for Eyelid Surgery?

Most often, blepharoplasty performed for women after the age of 40 years, but sometimes blepharoplasty is performed, and for younger patients.

This is due to some peculiarities of the lower eyelid and the appearance of small bags under the eyes at a young age, which are increases with time.

Just, more and more men have resorted to the eyelid surgery to give to the face a “fresh” look.

Blepharoplasty upper eyelid is performed for patients Asian appearance with , make the eyes more European if they wish.

Indications for eyelid surgery are:

  • overhanging skin of the upper eyelids;
  • fat protrusion of the upper eyelids combined with an excess of skin;
  • bags under the eyes;
  • bags under the eyes, combined with excess skin on the lower eyelids;
  • correction the shape of the eyes;
  • excess skin around the eyes;
  • “Asian” type of eye;
  • fatty hernia “bags” under the eyes.

Blepharoplasty is performed under local anesthesia, but at the request of patients can be carried out under general anesthesia.

The duration of baggy eye surgery is from 40 minutes to 2 hours, depending on the complexity of the correction eyelids. The eyeball is not affected during surgery.

Patients can leave the clinic after 2-3 hours after surgery.

The upper blepharoplasty.

The essence of the eyelid uplift is to perform a skin incision on the upper eyelid along the natural folds, making the scar is almost invisible. At the same time, excess skin of the upper eyelid is removed, which is marked before plastic surgery for eyelids. Excess skin on the upper eyelid is excised in a fusiform skin flap.

When performing blepharoplasty on the upper eyelid, in some cases, you may need to remove excess fat bags, as well as perform resection of the circular muscles of the eye, which makes the eyes more expressive and attractive.

Asian Blepharoplasty Surgery Philadelphia, Pennsylvania Day 5

We use radio wave device to perform blepharoplasty which perform precise incisions with minimal trauma, as well as minimizes the occurrence of edema and bruising.

This makes it possible to achieve excellent cosmetic results and reduce the recovery period. After performing the main stage, cosmetic sutures are imposed on the edges of the skin.

Lower blepharoplasty.

Open type blepharoplasty, ie a skin incision on the lower eyelid is used when excess upper eyelid skin. The incision is performed some distance of 1-2 mm of the lash line, which makes this incision is almost unnoticeable in the future.

It performs the mobilization of excess skin of the lower eyelid, the correction of fatty hernia on the lower eyelids.

Blepharoplasty 4 Days Post Op Philadelphia PA Pictures

The correction of the orbicularis oculi muscle can be performed if necessary to make the almond-shaped eyes. Then is performed excision of excess skin of the lower eyelid, and are superimposed cosmetic seams.

In cases where there is no excess skin on the lower eyelids, but there are bags under the eyes, is used the method of transconjunctival blepharoplasty in Philadelphia.

After anesthesia is performed incision of conjunctiva on the inner surface of the eyelid. At the same time surgeon is perform the removal of excess fat bags. Then the incision does not sewn, but simply clump together, and after 3-4 days is almost completely healed. Transconjunctival blepharoplasty, particularly prescribed for younger patients who have excess skin and want to get rid of bags under the eyes.

This type of blepharoplasty does not remain scars, significantly reduced postoperative recovery, as well as there is no violation of facial expressions and the shape of the eyes does not change.

The combination of the upper and lower blepharoplasty

In some cases, possible to perform combination of upper blepharoplasty with one type of Philadelphia lower blepharoplasty, which makes it possible to correct and upper and lower eyelids for one time. Correction of the upper eyelids in patients with “Asian eyes”.

Currently, many patients with the Asian eye shape want to become more like Europeans. For this purpose, the correction is performed of the upper eyelid and skin folds in the inner corner of the eye.

At the same time the upper eyelid becomes more pronounced shape and the eyes acquire a European look.

The postoperative period and recovery.

The recovery period is an average of 10-12 days. In some cases, on the eyelids can be formed small bruising and swelling which often disappear within 7-10 days. After the surgery on eyelids, a cold compress are superimposed to reduce swelling and bruising.

After surgery for eyelids for 3-5 days is recommended to apply the special eye drops, which also contributes to the rapid recovery. Sutures are removed on the 4-5th day.

Swelling on the eyelids gradually go away in 2-3 weeks. Within a month after the surgery on eyelids is necessary to exclude the intense physical activity and sauna.
