How long is the usual time to take off work after a mommy makeover?
Depends on your work
Mar 3, 2020
After a mommy makeover, I usually tell patients a minimum of 10 days-2 weeks if you have light duty or desk type work.  If work is more strenuous I recommend 2-3 weeks total time off. I like patients to return to light activity, meaning walking and getting heart rate up, as soon as possible as I feel it speed the healing process, just nothing strenuous.
PRP for hair loss. My doctor won't administer anesthesia before the procedure. Is this normal?
anesthetic
Mar 3, 2020
I have personally found PRP scalp injections to be quit painful.  We  use a combination of local anesthetic and Pronox (nitrous oxide) to make it tolerable.  With this routine patients rarely complain of significant discomfort.
Is it normal to feel depressed after Neograft surgery?
This is totally normal!
Dec 18, 2019
Hello there- no need to worry!  Feelings of decreased energy and sadness are common after any surgical procedures.  Many stress hormones are released during the trauma of a procedure and it can take a week or two to feel normal again.  Energy levels after larger surgeries can take a month or more to return.  So go easy on yourself and know this is totally normal!
When is it safe to restart low level laser therapy (LLLT) after 2500 FUE Neograft procedure?
LLLT
Dec 18, 2019
As others have said, there is no consensus of thought on restarting LLLT after transplant, but I would say above all, don't touch the grafts.  The LLLT itself is likely safe starting the day after the procedure.
Chin Augmentation ~ Results you can Expect?
many good answers
Dec 18, 2019
You have received many accurate and complete answers below.  However, I can add a different technique to consider. For modest increases in chin projection I use a non-surgical technique using a permanent filler called Belllafill.  It will stimulate your body to create its own tissue and can be done incrementally to achieve just the result you desire non-surgically.  I still do implants, but fewer these days.

Thank you for the question.  As others have said here, banana rolls are difficult to treat because if you remove enough skin to make them disappear when standing, when you flex your hip, you will widen the scar or worse limit your motion.  An in person consultation is the best way to proceed here as each person's skin quality differs and so too would the recommended treatments.

LLLT for hair loss. What is the difference between LEDs and Laser Diodes?
home light treatments
Dec 3, 2019
I won't reiterate what others have said before except to emphasize that consumer purchased devices will have lower power as compared to professional units.  However, the key to these therapies is consistency.  One must use them routinely to see benefits and often it is hard to develop a routine that incorporates these treatments.  Also, as others have said, combination treatments with PRP, rogaine, or finesteride  will show the best results.  
What is the success rate of FUE Neograft transplant in women with thin hair and balding?
neograft success
Oct 9, 2019
In my practice women and men experience a high success rate (graft survival around 90-95%).  The key is to make sure any underlying medical/ hormonal issues are controlled and then to supplement hair growth with other strategies like PRP (platlet rich plasma) and topicals as indicated.   The results can be profound.
How close under my eyes can Sciton Microlaser peel treat? Will it help with my acne scarring, smile
MLP
Aug 13, 2019
A micro laser peel can be done up to the eyelash margin but requires laser proof corneal shields.I like this procedure very much for fine lines of lower eyelids but it is an advanced technique and should only be done by a doctor who knows how to properly ensure corneal protection.
Can I get the Halo procedure on my face for acne scars if I have a tan on my face?
sun avoidance
Aug 13, 2019
UV light exposure causes melanocyte activity and pigment release.  Before any energy based treatments such as HALO, I strongly recommend sun protection/ avoidance for a week or 2 before the procedure, but strictly for at least 2 weeks afterwards to reduce the risk of post procedure complications.
Halo laser for acne scars?
acne scar treatment
Aug 13, 2019
I find that the best results come from combination therapy.  I use some/ all of the following treatments:RF microneedling, subcision with PRP, HALO, Fractional CO2, and bellafill for filling.I customize this for each patient depending on the scar type and skin type.  If you are darker skin toned, you might be better served with RF microneedling as a mainstay of treatment.
Is there any risk that Halo laser will permanently remove beard hair?
no concerns
Aug 13, 2019
HALO does not penetrate deep enough to permanently damage the hair follicles and its wavelength does not target pigment.  You'll be fine with your HALO treatment over beard areas.
Halo BBL. Should I keep trusting my doctor?
PIH
Aug 13, 2019
I have unfortunately seen this after HALO.  I agree topical hydroquinone and retinols can help resolution of PIH.  However, BBL would be my next treatment step to correct the residual pigment trapped in the epidermis. It sounds like your doctor is on the right track!
