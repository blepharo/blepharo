Implant size
Jul 8, 2020
I prefer to take measurements, but everyone has their own way of doing things.  Just looking at your photos, I think that something in the 450cc range would be reasonable to get you where you want to be.  It's always a good idea to get a few opinions as you are doing and go with whomever you feel most comfortable.  Simulators are somewhat helpful to some in choosing a size as well.
Would 720 grams per breast allow me to be a size DD from a 36H?
Breast reduction
Jun 16, 2020
Cup sizes vary widely among manufacturers, so that can be very difficult to pin down.  There is a maximum safe amount that can be resected without jeopardizing the blood supply to the nipple, so one must be careful about trying to go too small.  750 grams seems like a reasonable goal for you based on your photos, and maybe even a little more.  The nipple piercing should not be an issue.
What is the best incision if I'm prone to keloid scars and interested in getting a breas
Scar
Jun 16, 2020
Unfortunately if you are prone to thickened scars, any incision may leave an unfavorable scar.  The key to minimizing scars is minimizing the tension placed on those scars.  The incision in the fold tends to be under very little tension and usually heals very favorably.  It is also the least visible if it doesn't end up healing well.
Drain or no drain for a tummy tuck?
Tummy tuck
May 12, 2020
Whether or not a surgeon uses drains or some other means of closing off the space that is created during the procedure is a matter of personal preference.  There is good data to support either method.  Find a surgeon with good results that connects with you.  That is far more important than their shoice of surgical technique.
How long can I drive after BR surgery?
Driving after surgery
May 12, 2020
This is a discussion best had with your plastic surgeon as everyone has their own opinions on the matter.  You definitely should not be driving if taking any narcotics or if it hurts to put your hands on the steering wheel.  There is unfortunately no real way to predict your level of post-op discomfort ahead of time.  Talk to your surgeon and make a plan togethre.
I am 5'9", athletic build, 140 lbs, deciding if 520cc high profiles will be too big?
Implant size
May 12, 2020
You are correct to realize that this is an important decision.  The size of your implants can affect not only your appearance, but also your mobility and back strain in active people.  A simulator can be helpful in making the choice, as can trying on sizers, but the best way is a discussion with your surgeon about your goals.  Based on your measurments and your goals, the best implant can be chosen.
Can you get a Mommy Makeover and an arm lift at the same time?
Combined procedures
May 6, 2020
While it is possible to have all three done at once, that is a bit more in one procedure than I would be comfortable doing.  Getting around after a tummy tuck is difficult, so I prefer that patients are able to use their arms normally.  Anything that further limits mobility increases risk, as does the increased surgical time.  I would personally recommend staging the procedures, at least into two steps.

Saggy neck at 35. How can I fix this?
Neck lift
Apr 17, 2020
The gold standard for treating the amount of skin laxity that you appear to have is a formal necklift.  Liposuction will not correct the issue and could potentially exacerbate it.  If you are trying to avoid surgery, then a threadlift such as Eurothreads could be used to diminish the laxity.  Best to meet with a plastic surgeon to create realistic expectations.
Are my breast tuberous/constricted? What can be done to correct this without implants? (
Tuberous breasts
Apr 17, 2020
You have a very mild tuberous deformity of the breasts.  This could be corrected with an incision around the areola.  Augmentation could be performed with fat grafting to the breasts and would simultaneously enhance your waistline due to the liposuction for fat collection.  Moving the nipples toward the center is not generally practical due to the scars necessary to accomplish that.  What can and cannot be accomplished is best discussed with an in person consult with a plastic surgeon.
