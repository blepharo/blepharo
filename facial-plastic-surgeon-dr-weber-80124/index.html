What is the best treatment to prevent scarring from my head injury?
Oct 12, 2017
For now: keep the wound and sutures moist and clean with antibiotic ointment. Sutures: make sure that your sutures are removed between 5-7 days (I prefer 5 days)After suture removal: Consider a scar gel such as Stratamed or Silagen6-8 weeks post injury: Consider laser resurfacing or dermabrasion. Dermabrasion of the forehead can occasionally lead to prolonged redness so laser is likely more beneficial.Best.
