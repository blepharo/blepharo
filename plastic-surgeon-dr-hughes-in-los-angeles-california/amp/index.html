I would ask the board certified plastic surgeon or other specialist who assisted you in gaining approval for the panniculectomy, TT, and lipo. Should I pursue getting a panniculectomy through my insurance, or am I not a good candidate? (Photo)
Jul 13, 2020
The panniculectomy involves only the removal of excess skin/fat that hangs over the genitals and/or thighs. It does address hygiene issues, rashes/infections, etc. When a panniculectomy is done, there is no tightening of the underlying muscles and tapering of the waist so you do not get the contour shape. Panniculectomy is only the removal of the skin below the belly button, so the best aesthetic results may be attained with a TT.
