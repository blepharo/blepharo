Pittsburgh, PA Eyelid Raise Surgery Prices

One of the early signs of skin aging is its overhanging and the appearance of wrinkles in the upper and lower eyelids, so Blepharoplasty of upper and lower eyelids in Pittsburgh is an operation with which many women begin their “trip” on plastic surgery.

The first on what we pay attention when meeting with acquaintances or strangers humans are the eyes.

In the first place we look “eyes to eyes”, only then our view falls on the middle third of the face and neck. For this reason, eyelid surgery is one of the most popular and sought-after operation in plastic surgery.

Blepharoplasty helps to eliminate age-related changes eyelids and congenital structural features of the upper and lower eyelids.

Pittsburgh blepharoplasty is divided into upper and lower blepharoplasty, if necessary, both operations are performed simultaneously.

What is the mechanism of occurrence of age-related changes the eyelids?

Our eyes are placed in the eye sockets. The eye sockets are formed of the skull bones. Between the eye and the eye socket is a special fat, which performs the function of shock absorbers for our eyes.

But gradually the fat tissue is squeezed out of the eye socket to the upper and lower eyelids area. There are so-called “bags” in the eyelid area. In the future, at loss of skin elasticity eyelids appear are numerous wrinkles and excess skin, all this gives a tired expression on your face.

Blepharoplasty of upper eyelids is a minimally invasive surgery, the main thing in it – the ability of the surgeon to perform the incisions, to postoperative scar with eyes open was not visible, it should be located in a natural depression in the upper eyelids.

Blepharoplasty of lower eyelids in Pittsburgh PA is a much more complex operation, in view features of the skin to contract and pull down the lower eyelid, what leads to the rounding of the eyes.

This operation is aimed at removing fat hernias (bags under the eyes). Wrinkles and excess skin, of course, will decreased, but it can not removed completely, as this will lead to a sagging of the lower eyelid and to the rounding eyes.

For the correction of wrinkles in these areas after blepharoplasty should be used dermabrasion, peeling and Botox injections.

With good elasticity of the skin the lower eyelids is used transconjunctival blepharoplasty, in which the fatty hernia is removed without an incision in the lower eyelid area through minimal punctures in conjunctivitis.

The surgery for eyelids is unique in that never happens an ectopia (eversion of the lower eyelid edge), and changing the shape of the eye.

How is eyelid surgery performed?

Most surgeons during operations on the upper eyelids use the incisions, reminiscent of sickle or ellipse shape. The surgeon removes fat herniation, after excision of excess skin.

Intradermal suture is superimposed after surgery. In the lower eyelids area are usually used the incisions from one corner of the eye to the other along the edge of the eyelid indented from it for 1-2 mm.

Intradermal suture is also superimposed after the excision of excess skin and removal fat hernias.

Postoperative period is painless. During the first two days may disturbed occurrence of conjunctivitis. Usually, the next day after surgery sutures are removed and on the wounds are superimposed fixing strips (gluing) for 6-7 days. After removing the strips, you can be applied makeup that hided traces of the cosmetic surgery eyes.

Preoperative preparation.

Blepharoplasty does not require special preparation.

Anesthesia: local or general anesthesia.

Duration of surgery: upper or lower blepharoplasty takes from 40 minutes to 1 hour; The circular (upper and lower), blepharoplasty can take up to 2 hours.

The recovery period.

Sutures are removed on day 3-4. Bruising around the eyes are disappearing within 1-2 weeks, depending on volume of surgery for eyelids and health of the patient.

Hospital stay: not required.

The duration of the effect of the eyelid surgery in Pittsburgh is not less than 10 years.
