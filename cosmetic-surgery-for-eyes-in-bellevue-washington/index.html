What problems are not corrected by blepharoplasty?

There are a number of aesthetic deficiencies that with age or an anatomically develop in the upper third of the face – around the eyes and eyebrows.

Nevertheless, despite the fact that they are located in the periorbital area, to correct it with blepharoplasty is not possible. Let’s consider them in order.

The skin condition and facial wrinkles.

Rejuvenating surgery on the eyelids can not solve the problems of the skin condition. That is, during eyelid surgery can talk about reducing the pronounced folds on the eyelids, on the reduction of apparent excess skin, but a small facial wrinkles (so-called “crow’s feet”), the structure of the skin will not be affected during the traditional blepharoplasty.

Preparations containing botulin are used to remove fine facial wrinkles, which are able to block the muscle, thereby depriving it of the possibility of contraction and provoke the formation of wrinkles.

If the wrinkles were already formed, they should be filled hyaluronic fillers. The problems associated with the quality of the skin on the eyelids should be resolved with the help of laser therapy, peels and a combination of these procedures with lipofilling of the eyelids.

Tear trough.

Washington Blepharoplasty 40 Year Old

Modern blepharoplasty in Bellevue offers not to remove, and redistribute fat that protrudes in the lower eyelids area. However, it is evident that depth of a tear trough gets larger with age, and fat, which is in the fat hernia of the lower eyelid is not enough for filling and leveling of the lower eyelid area / cheekbones. Therefore, the best solution is lipofilling lower eyelids.

Dark circles under the eyes.

The reasons for the formation of dark circles under the eyes can be a lot. However, if their occurrence is not associated with the development of complex diseases, they lend themselves well to lipofiling.

Bags under the eyes (sufa).

Bags are formed under the lower eyelids in the cheeks area. They are often confused with a herniated lower eyelid because of the proximity. The nature of their formation and location on the face is that getting rid of this problem possible only with the help of other operations: it’s cheek lift is performed through an incision on the lower eyelid and often combined with the plastic of the lower eyelid.

Or it is possible to use a less traumatic way – lipofilling of the cheekbones that will allow to camouflage unevenness in the cheeks and lower eyelids, recreate the correct facial architecture.

The lowering of the lateral part of the upper eyelid.

Sometimes in the course of examination and diagnosis revealed that the excess skin on the upper eyelid appeared due to sagging of the lateral edge of the eyebrow.

Then the patient is recommended to lift eyebrows. Often the problem can be solved with the help of the lipofilling, when the eyebrow occupies its correct anatomic position by restoring volume.

What Eyelid Surgery Technique Doesn’t Require Stitches at All

Often, talking about blepharoplasty without incisions, we mean Transconjunctival blepharoplasty of the lower eyelid. The surgeon made the incision on the inside of the lower eyelid, and after the eye cosmetic surgery the traces is not noticeable to the others, such as seams or scars. One of the main problems of patients seeking about blepharoplasty are bags under the eyes.

Male Blepharoplasty In Bellevue Washington

These patients are not aware and often do not see that a problem exists on another level, and this problem – tear trough.

The logic of standard blepharoplasty is a decision to remove a hernia in the lower eyelids to the level of tear trough. As a result, the patient receives or dark circles under the eyes, or, what is worse – the retraction of the lower eyelids and unaesthetic deepening under the eyes.

In our view, the approach should be just the opposite. Adding volume to the area under the eyes allows not only camouflages the hernia due to alignment differences levels hernia and depression tear trough, but also to ensure a rejuvenation of the lower eyelid and skin because fat, which we use for lipofilling contains a large number of growth factors, which are promote skin rejuvenation eyelids.
