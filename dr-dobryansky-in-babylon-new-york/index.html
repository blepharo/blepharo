Should I get a forehead reduction, hairline lowering or a different procedure to achieve a
Forehead reduction
Jul 4, 2020
Thank you for your question. It’s a great one. This is more about the skin itself than hair. A forehead reduction would involve a hairline incision. The best way to prevent scar related complications such as hypertrophy, is to break up the scar into multiple curves. This decreases tension on the closure. I also recommend laser scar treatment shortly after the surgery and possibly another 6 weeks down the line.
