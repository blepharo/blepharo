Smart or vaser liposuction?
Difference between Vaser and Smart Liposuction
Oct 8, 2019
Smart Lipo uses a very small fiber that allows us to go in between the skin and muscle where the fat is located and heat the fat and melt it. The fat should come out a little bit easier and you can get the skin tighter as well. On the other hand Smart Lipo heats in an even plane and offers more of an even removal of the fat. Best, Dr. Edward Dickerson
