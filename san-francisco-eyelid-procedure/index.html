Beautiful eyes are the subject of admiration throughout the world. Unfortunately, sometimes, sagging eyelids, bags under the eyes can overshadow the brilliance streamy from the beautiful eyes, because with age, the skin of the lower eyelid droops beneath it formed “fat” hernia, eyebrows stronger hanging over the the eye sockets, and the beauty of the whole face fades due these adverse changes.

Fortunately, though nature has made the aging process irreversible, plastic surgery can help to correct the unpleasant changes in the eyelid area.

Today it is possible to make a blepharoplasty (eyelid surgery). So, what exactly is blepharoplasty? Blepharoplasty in San FranciscoÂ is a cosmetic procedure that usually included in the complex process, referred to as a facelift.

But along with the patient’s wishes can be considered as a separate procedure.

There are four types of blepharoplasty:

– Blepharoplasty of upper eyelids helps eliminate the upper eyelid changes.
– Blepharoplasty of lower eyelids is responsible for the the transformation of the lower eyelid.
– Transconjunctival lower lid blepharoplasty is the surgery of the lower eyelids without seams.
– Circular blepharoplasty is a one-stage correction of the upper and lower eyelids.

People with sagging eyelid skin is recommended to lower blepharoplasty, a small incision is made along the lash line in this type of intervention, through which fatty tissue and excess skin is are removed.

A small seam remains after this procedure, which is resorbed in three weeks and becomes completely invisible under the lashes.

Like any other medical procedure, blepharoplasty have contraindications, the main of which is a rapidly progressive loss of vision.

In all other cases, patients can immediately do this procedure after passing the necessary tests and consultations.

But we should not forget that the swelling under the eyes can also occur on a more serious reason, before resorting to cosmetic eyelid surgery procedure, it is necessary carry out a full examination, and carefully examine the kidneys, thyroid gland, and a predisposition to allergies.

The advantage of modern plastic surgery is the ability to quickly return to the usual lifestyle. Sutures are removed on the third day after blepharoplasty. Within a month, you will be advised to limit playing sport and visiting the baths and saunas, as well as to refrain from salty, spicy food and plentiful drink. As for smoking, it has a negative effect on the complexion, so from this bad habit is to refuse, to preserve the beauty for years to come.
