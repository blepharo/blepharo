Surgery For Eyelids In Virginia Beach, VA

Female view is a weapon, which all women possess, so beautiful and fit eyelids is a guarantee that you will be noticed.

It is no secret that with age, the eyelids are lowered, and to preserve the beauty and expressiveness of the eyes people do eyelid surgery.

Almost all the stars, politicians and film actors resorted to blepharoplasty in Virginia Beach.

Blepharoplasty is an operation that allows rejuvenate the upper and lower eyelids to create a pleasant aesthetic effect.

With this operation is possible to get rid excess skin and fat hernias (bags under the eyes), to change the shape of the eyes and eyelids, as well as get rid of the existing defects.

Types of operation:

Circular blepharoplasty – circular lifting upper and lower eyelids;
Upper Blepharoplasty – lifting of the upper eyelids;
Lower Blepharoplasty – lifting the lower eyelids;
Transconjunctival blepharoplasty in Virginia Beach is an operation without incisions, which is performed for people up to 30 years if not much pronounced defects of eyelids (performed on the lower eyelids). You must have a personal consultation for a successful cosmetic surgery eye lift, which can help determine its peculiarities in your particular case.

Blepharoplasty in Virginia Beach VA is performed under local anesthesia in the case of surgery of the upper eyelids and under general anesthesia during surgery lower eyelids, so breakfast is permitted only with surgery of the upper eyelids in a day of operation.

When general anesthesia on the day of surgery in the morning, you can not eat and drink. As soon as the patient goes in the hospital, it is placed in a special ward, where the plastic surgeon makes marks on the face, at the location where the eyelid lifting will be carried.

Premedication is carried out after the marking. This procedure allows to avoid infectious complications after plastic surgery for eyelids. The bottom eyelid surgery is performed in a sterile room under local anesthetic in the case of operations on the upper eyelids and general anesthesia if the operation is carried out on the lower eyelids.

During the blepharoplasty in Virginia Beach, we carefully monitoring the symmetry, so the surgeon continuously checks and evaluates the identity of the removed areas of the skin and fatty hernia on the right and left.

Firstly, surgeon performs eyelid uplift on the one side and then on the other. In the case of classical eyelid surgery – are superimposed special sutures that allow to close the place of the surgical intervention (stitches are removed 3-5 days after eye cosmetic surgery).

Recovery after blepharoplasty

The recovery process after blepharoplasty of the upper and lower eyelids are slightly different: When the operation is performed on the upper eyelids, the recovery period takes 5-10 days, but the timing of the rehabilitation period is exclusively individual.

We encourage our patients to come at the control inspection of at 1 month after cosmetic surgery eye lift. If the operation is performed on the lower eyelids, the recovery period takes 14-16 days, but the timing of the rehabilitation period is exclusively individual.

The final results of blepharoplasty can be estimated not earlier than 6 months. If you need a slight correction, it is usually carried out in the same timeframe. In most patients, postoperative scars invisible, the results of blepharoplasty stored in an average of 7-10 years.

Contraindications to blepharoplasty

In addition to somatic diseases, impeding the realization of any plastic surgery, the contraindications to perform blepharoplasty is the presence of acute and chronic inflammatory diseases of the conjunctiva, the lacrimal ducts and glands, dry eye syndrome, excessive swelling around the eyes, winking spasm, hyperthyroidism and other diseases of the eye.

Risks and complications of blepharoplasty

The biggest fears of after blepharoplasty are associated with patient dissatisfaction with the results of the operation. Side effects of blepharoplasty include a slight change in shape of the eyes, its rounding, and therefore increases the visible part of the whites of the eyes.

If a strong tension of the lower eyelid due to removal during blepharoplasty of the excess skin can occur omitted corners of the eyes, which gives despondent, sad look. In the case of the initially low tone of the lower eyelid, or removed excess skin from it, may occur ectropion, which disappears on their own within six months. When saving ectropion 6 months after blepharoplasty may required the repeated surgery for surgical correction of this complication.

Septic complications, bleeding, blurred vision and eye cornea injury after blepharoplasty are found only rarely. In the first weeks after blepharoplasty are formed swelling and bruising in the eyes area.

It should be noted that the aesthetic results of blepharoplasty and their duration are largely dependent on the behavior of the patient. The result of blepharoplasty will be long-lasting with proper the skin care of eyelids and maintaining of general health.
