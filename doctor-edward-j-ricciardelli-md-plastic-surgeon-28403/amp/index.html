What technique would be done to make my nose thinner , more feminine?
Round nose
Jan 22, 2020
Although your nose may appear lare and round to you, attempts to make the nose smaller by taking tissue out is a mistake.  You need more support not less.  You need tip support with grafting and suturing to give better definition to the tip and dorsum of the nose.  Yes, minor excisions of tissue in the nostrils and some of the underlying cartilage can be done but adding support to your nose is important.  Find a surgeon well versed in these techniques.  Good luck
