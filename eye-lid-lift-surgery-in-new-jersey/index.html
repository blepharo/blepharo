Transcutaneous lid blepharoplasty in New Jersey.

The excess skin under the eyes is removed by incision just below the lower lashes. At the same time fat tissue is eliminated, redistributed, or added, the incision can be used to perform a face lift. If too much skin was removed, the lower eyelid goes down or can even occur ectropion, the eyes looks more round and the white of the eye stands out. This can be prevented, if to perform canthoplasty.

SOOF (SUB ORBICULARIS OCULI FAT).

Soof is a fatty deposit under the lower eyelid at the top of the cheek. Soof lift raises the fat and thus fills the cavity in the upper part of the cheek or eye. Soof sometimes replaced with facelift, but the latter is more extensive and with the help of fat tissue, muscles, and sometimes even the skin are transplanted. Soof can be performed by making an incision through the skin or inside the lower eyelid.

Canthopexy and Canthoplasty.

Canthopexy is the name of the procedure which is performed to elevate the outer corners of the lids. The lateral Canthoplasty is a similar procedure with similar purpose.

The difference between them is that Canthopexy is the connection of the corner in a higher position, while Canthoplasty is the connection of the corner at a higher position after this has been detached from its previous attachment point.

Functional blepharoplasty.

If the skin, sagging through the corner of the eye, closes the eye and interferes with vision, then functional blepharoplasty will remove it and, thus, improve the function of the eye.

Functional blepharoplasty in New Jersey refers to reconstructive surgery and the costs are covered by the insurance company. Before the operation it is necessary to undergo an examination and have the direction of the ophthalmologist with you.

Swelling. The skin around the eyes is very prone to edema, and quite a small amount of extra fluid can cause extensive swelling. We recommend that you removable cold compresses in the first 24 – 48 hours after eyelid skin removal in NJ (20 minutes) and with a raised head and chest during sleep for localization swelling and speedy recovery. In order to prevent asymmetry is recommended sleeping on back at least the first week after eyelid removal surgery.

Do not tilt the head below the shoulders, if you want to pick something up from the floor, better sit down. In the area around the eyes is recommended to perform lymphatic drainage, the doctor can show to you or refer to the a trained beautician.

Bruises.

Because the skin on the eyelids is very thin and has blood vessels, then any operation around the eyes will have bruises. They gradually disappear within a week. Seams. If you have sutures that must be removed, it will make your surgeon after 3 – 5 days after hooded lid surgery.

Do not rub the scars after removal of sutures after removing the stitches, if the seams are on the inside of the eyelid, then they will absorbed in a few days. Internal seams can irritate the eyes and cause lacrimation and redness. In this case, the doctor has to remove them, but these problems can be a sign of infection.

Recovery after lid lift surgery is painless, but the doctor may prescribe analgesics, which you will take, if necessary.

Results.

After one to two weeks swelling subsides and you can evaluate the results of the operation after about six weeks. If you have undergone canthopexy, the swelling and the feeling of firmness around the eyes will take a little longer.
