Beverly Hills Surgical Eye Lift

Eyelid surgery, or blepharoplasty, in Beverly Hills is the choice of thousands of men and women, aimed at correction “bags” in the skin folds around the eyes.

Although more women than men are choosing blepharoplasty, surgery may equally be performed and for men. People of all ages are undergoing surgery.

Younger patients often have surgery because of congenital deformations in the form of “edema”, “bags” under the eyes.

Wrinkles and hanging skin folds are added to these changes in the elderly people.

The overhanging eyelids can make you older, and in some cases even violate eyesight and to limit the field of vision due to skin folds.

Beverly Hills Blepharoplasty allows to correct the these age-related changes, giving the face a more youthful and fresh appearance.

During the consultation, the surgeon asks questions about vision, lachrymation use of contact lenses, and also that the patient expects from the surgery.

This information, the patient’s age, skin type, degree of impairment, are evaluated by determining the best treatment option in each case.

Eyelid surgery is usually not completely removes the wrinkles around the eyes and does not lift overhanging eyebrow.

Therefore, many patients decide to combine blepharoplasty with other rejuvenating operations in the forehead and the face, thus achieving the best possible result.

Dr. Kimberly J. Lee Eyelid Surgery

Blepharoplasty is often performed under general anesthesia in a hospital environment. Typically, the cosmetic surgery eyes lasts from 1 to 1.5 hours. The upper and lower blepharoplasty can be performed simultaneously, but each operation should be done as a separate operation.

Upper Blepharoplasty is performed through an incision in the natural crease of the eyelid skin. Excess skin and fat are removed and the incision is closed with intradermal cosmetic suture. Thus, the visibility of the scar becomes minimal.

When the lower blepharoplasty, the incision is along lash line in the natural folds of the skin arising during a smile.

At the end of corrective eyelid surgery, the surgeon sutured the wound with an intradermal cosmetic seam.

If necessary, especially in the elderly, the classic lower eyelids surgery can be supplemented with lateral canthopexy, ie the strengthening of the muscles that hold the eyelash edge of the eyelid and the eyelid.

In younger patients, if there is no excess skin, removal of “fatty bags” can be performed using transconjunctival blepharoplasty, that is, inside the eyelid, resulting in the formation of completely undetectable scar.

Recovery

Most patients are quite easy to tolerate blepharoplasty in Beverly Hills, feeling a slight discomfort that can eliminate by medication. After eye lid lift, the patient is given the necessary recommendations for the care of the operated area.

Swelling and temperate bleeding is usually present after eyelid surgery. Cold compresses, sleep with your head elevated, physiotherapy can help accelerate healing and reduce discomfort. Sutures are removed after 3-4 days after cosmetic surgery under eyes.

The ultraviolet radiation may slightly enhance the pigmentation of scars, so sunscreen, glasses and hats are desirable using during the formation of scar. Aerobics and other physical activities should be excluded for 3 weeks.

The first few days after cosmetic surgery on eyes may experience sealings, reduced sensitivity and a certain painfulness to the touch eyelids in the healing process. These changes are usually disappear within a few weeks or months after surgical eye lift.
