Prices Of Plastic Surgery On Eyelids In Temecula, CA

When we meet a stranger or a child in the first place to draw attention to the eyes. It is said that the eyes are the mirror of the soul, they are certainly attract attention.

But, unfortunately, by nature, not all people have symmetrically and beautiful eyes. Often, even the young people are not satisfied to the eyes shape, ptosis of the upper eyelid, asymmetry, puffiness and dark circles around the eyes.

The most effective solution to this problem is blepharoplasty in Temecula. This operation solves any problems: eliminates the “bags under the eyes,” the overhang of the upper eyelid, narrow eyes shape and even smoothes out wrinkles.

Laser Eyelid Surgery will make your eyes expressive and beautiful. Laser blepharoplasty procedure performed on Smart Xide DOT equipment (DEKA).

Laser Blepharoplasty generally takes place once in life and solves various problems of the eyelids forever: remove hernias of the lower eyelids, which form the swelling and “bags” under the eyes, upper eyelids are tightened, smoothed out the wrinkles around the eyes.

You need blepharoplasty in Temecula, if:

– there is an overhang of the upper eyelids;
– puffiness and “bags” under the eyes;
– wrinkles around the eyes;
– corners of the eyes are omitted downwards.

Often blepharoplasty procedure in Temecula is carried out simultaneously on the upper and lower eyelids, this operation is called a circular lifting of the eyelids.

When circular blepharoplasty, the surgeon removes excess tissue (skin) on the upper and lower eyelid, as well as excess fat tissue.

1 Week After Upper Eyelid Surgery Patient Photo Results By Doctor Amir M. Karam

In some cases, the lower eyelids do not have excess tissue, but there is puffiness under the eyes, it is due to the fact that fat deposits (fat tissue) accumulate in the lower eyelids area.

In this case, is performed transconjunctival blepharoplasty of the lower eyelids. Under local anesthesia, an incision is made through the mucosa of the lower eyelid (conjunctiva), whereupon by laser removes excess fatty deposits.

At the time the procedure, a special lens protects the eyeball, so the risk of eye damage is excluded and the procedure is a safe.

Contraindications to laser blepharoplasty:

  1. dry eye;
  2. diabetes;
  3. oncological diseases;
  4. intraocular pressure;
  5. bleeding disorders;
  6. pregnancy;
  7. Lactation.

Procedure Laser Eyelid Surgery (Blepharoplasty) is carried out by means of hot SmartXide DOT CO2 laser. Due to the fact, that the skin incision is carried out using CO2 laser, during eye sagging surgery takes place a blood clotting, so the eye surgery for droopy eyelids takes place without loss of blood.

Dr Bobby S. Korn Eyelid Sagging Surgery

Recovery lasts three times faster, than when classical blepharoplasty (using a scalpel, without using a laser).

During the eye surgery plastic, the risk of infection is excluded, because the laser is killing all bacteria, without bruising, swelling after the bottom eyelid surgery, the operation and the recovery period is easily tolerated. A full recovery takes about 1-2 weeks.

How is the procedure of laser blepharoplasty performed?

Eyelid Surgery in Temecula is performed in a sterile operating by the surgeon under local anesthesia: in the area around the eyes is injected a local anesthetic, the procedure is performed after anesthesia.

During eye tightening surgery, the patient feels no pain, the procedure lasts for only 30-120 minutes, depending on the type of blepharoplasty (upper or lower). The skin around the eyes is completely restored within 7-14 days.
