What are the after-effects of multiple mole removals?
Raised moles in any location are usually best removed with excision. Small flat moles can be removed with laser.
Jan 30, 2020
A careful, skillful excision of raised moles is usually the best permanent method for mole removal with the best cosmetic result and the not risk of recurrence.  Small flat moles less than 3mm in diameter can be removed with a laser with excellent cosmetic results.   The final appearance depends on the skill of the physician and post operative care. 
What is the best way to remove flat moles on face?
Raised Moles are best removed with a punch or elliptical excision. Small flat mole can be treated with a laser.
Jan 30, 2020
Raised moles are best excised to assure a complete removal and to get rid of the part of the mole that is in the dermis.  Small flat moles (junctional nevi) can be treated with a laser. If done correctly, this will leave little or no scar.  Using chemical and destructive methods for treating scars will often leave an unsightly scar and increase the risk of recurrence.
