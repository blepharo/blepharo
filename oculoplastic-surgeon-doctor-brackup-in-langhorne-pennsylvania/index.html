Would a face lift make me look look odd?
Facelift?
Jun 2, 2020
Thanks for your question and your photos. I don't think a facelift in itself if done correctly would make you look "odd". I think you need to be very clear with your surgeon specifically what you want to address. Possibly some doctors do not think they can address your concerns through a lift so they refuse. I think managing expectations and the type of result you desire will be most beneficial before deciding on any procedure. I hope this helps. 
Do facelifts performed in your 30s age poorly?
Facelift?
Jun 2, 2020
Great question... and honestly I haven't heard or read anything that supports the claim of an early facelift interfering with aging. Once the skin is manually lifted you will still continue to age. You may want to have another procedure done later in life, but that wont be because you had one now. I think that if after weight loss you have saggy skin that really bothers you its best to just get it fixed.

Will IPL effect injectable? An MD said he didn't want to do any fillers until my 3 IPL's are
IPL and Fillers
Mar 4, 2020
We do both of these treatments simultaneously... with a few days in between. If you have an IPL and get swollen for a few days you would want to wait until the swelling goes down prior to placing filler. Additionally we typically have patients wait 24-48 hours after fillers to have IPL done. I wouldn’t recommend finishing an entire series before adding filler. Hope this helps.
