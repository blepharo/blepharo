Hawaii Eyelid Surgery Blepharoplasty Prices

Eyelid surgery in Hawaii helps to significantly refresh the face, gives a rested look, removes age-related eyelid changes, get rid of “tired” eyes and “bags” under the eyes.

After all, the eyes determine the age of a person, therefore, it is no accident that the majority of patients who begin to pay attention to age-related changes in the face, initially refer to the surgery of the eyelids, in order to obtain a pronounced rejuvenating effect.

Preparation for eyelid surgery in Hawaii:

– Laboratory examination (tests);
– Consultation of the endocrinologist and gynecologist (if there is a puffiness and facial pigmentation);
– Consultation of a dermatologist-cosmetologist (in the presence of dry withering skin of eyelids and face);
– Consultation of an anesthesiologist (if the blepharoplasty treatment is planned under anesthesia).

Blepharoplasty of upper eyelids

Blepharoplasty of the upper eyelids in Hawaii forms an open semicircular fold of the upper eyelid, characteristic for a young age, removes the fatty hernia and overhanging fold of the upper eyelid, which greatly enhances the rejuvenating effect.

The upper blepharoplasty is performed under local anesthesia and lasts about 20-30 minutes, and in combination with the use of a laser for sealing the vessels. The blepharoplasty treatment does not leave bruises and retains the effect for a longer period. At the stage of preoperative marking, the surgeon determines the natural fold of the skin of the upper eyelid along which the cosmetic micro-suture will be made, therefore, it is not noticeable after healing.

Partial correction of the skin fold of the eyelid is performed with a slight overhanging. (occurs at a young age and is more a feature of the anatomical structure).

Correct The Upper And Lower Eyelids Sagging

Blepharoplasty of the lower eyelids.

Blepharoplasty of the lower eyelids will forever solve the problem of “bags” under the eyes, relieve wrinkles, and also make the skin of the eyelid more elastic. In the presence of only periorbital hernias and with a good skin turgor, transconjunctival blepharoplasty is performed from the internal access from the mucosa – without skin incisions.

Using techniques and special tests during blepharoplasty treatment allows to determine the amount of skin and fat removed hernias with high accuracy.

Removal of hernias with a laser prevents the appearance of bruising, forms a dense frame of the tissues of the eyelid, and increases the turgor of the skin, thus prolonging the result of the eye lid lift for 4-5 years.

After the eye surgery for droopy eyelids, a bandage is applied and eye drops are inserted, which prevent swelling. There are no pain sensations, bed rest is recommended within the next 2-3 hours.

The cosmetic micro-suture is removed for 4-5 days absolutely painlessly. Minor swelling disappears on the 6-7th day and you can return to everyday life. But according to the majority of patients, the final result is determined only the second – the third month later.

At this stage it is recommended to contact a cosmetologist for the appointment of a skin care complex for the eyelids (massage, masks, regenerative and moisturizing mesotherapy) and the carrying out of hardware programs – RF-lifting, thermage, etc.

Patients referring to plastic surgeons, often complain about skin folds, facial wrinkles and hernias of the lower eyelid. It is these shortcomings that are most noticeable on the face and visually add to the age.

How is laser blepharoplasty performed?

Today, the most effective method for solving these problems is laser transconjunctival blepharoplasty. The uniqueness of this method is that the incision is carried out on the inner side of the eyelid, conjunctiva, and the mucous membrane is quickly and well restored.

The use of a laser beam is not as traumatic as the use of standard surgical instruments, and does not cause bleeding. The laser technique is so successfully used for blepharoplasty of the lower eyelids also because there are no scars left on the operated area.

This operation, with rare exceptions, is performed under local anesthesia. The risk of complications with this method is also minimal.

Contraindications.

Contraindications for the eye tuck surgery can serve as any infection, serious chronic diseases (for example, thyroid gland hyperfunction, cancer, blood clotting disorders, diabetes mellitus), high intraocular pressure.

Recovery period.

After transconjunctival blepharoplasty in Hawaii, the patient’s eyelids will be protected for several days with a special plaster for early healing and reducing edema. Cold compresses should be used on the first day.

Edemas and bruises will begin to subside after 5-7 days after surgery for eyes – during this period you can not use cosmetics and should avoid physical exertion. Recovery period can take from two to three weeks, depending on the individual characteristics of the body.

The result of blepharoplasty.

The aesthetic effect of laser transconjunctival blepharoplasty of the lower eyelids can be seen almost immediately after the eyelid blepharoplasty. The view becomes open, wrinkles disappear, the skin under the eyes will become smoother.

The use of the laser avoids hematomas and forms a denser eyelid skeleton that guarantees the effect of the eye surgery plastic for many years with a proper lifestyle and observance of all the doctor’s recommendations.
