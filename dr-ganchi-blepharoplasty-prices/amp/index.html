Smoking Necklifts and Blepharoplasty Do Not Mix Well

In general for someone whom is currently smoking healing from a Facelift or neck lift is compromised. The vascularity of the skin flaps is not as robust as it could be and the result is usually somewhat compromised

However if one has stopped smoking for more than 2 weeks (6 weeks preferably) healing is similar to someone whom has not smoked. Congratulations on cessation of smoking!!! With regards to Blepharoplasty – smoking is not a contraindication as the vascularity is so generous in this area we have not seen it to be a problem with healing, however we always encourage our patients to be as healthy as possible prior to any procedure
