Recovery after eyelid surgery.

Most often, patient does not have severe pain after blepharoplasty, and there is no need for pain medications. The main sensations after the baggy eye surgery is discomfort in the operated area.

And that’s fine. The first 2-3 days after eye lid operation the swelling increases, then it starts to decrease. Most often recommend the use of cold compresses to the eye for 2-3 days after blepharoplasty.

If you feel pain and significant discomfort after cosmetic surgery eyes – please ask for help to your plastic surgeon.

Swelling and bruising is a usual phenomenon after the plastic surgery on eyelids. These temporary phenomena are expressed to a greater or lesser extent in each patient.

Mostly during the week bruising and swelling are significantly reduced and then completely disappear.

The first time after blepharoplasty, patients often feel the tension of the skin, sometimes uneven. The eyes do not close tightly. These phenomena are temporary, the skin eventually returns to normal, the scars, “relax” and tension is reduced.

The first time after the plastic eye surgery is strongly recommended not to disturb the operated area (do not rub or scratch the eye area). Do not use cosmetics.

If you experience dryness, burning or itching are allowed the use of special an eye drop prescribed by a doctor. A few weeks after blepharoplasty, you may notice increased lacrimation and sensitivity to light.

As a rule, during the recovery period, the patient comes on the periodically check ups to your surgeon. Sutures are removed 2-4 days and a special patch is pasted.

Only your doctor can comment the recovery progress, so any issues you should ask your doctor. The final result of blepharoplasty you will appreciate no earlier than 2 months after the plastic surgery on eyelids.

Possible complications after eyelid surgery.

Any surgical intervention may be accompanied by complications (during cosmetic eye lift and / or in the postoperative period). Most often, the surgeon’s experience plays an important role in such situations.

The physical condition of the patient, the presence of certain diseases can cause certain complications. Therefore, for any operation, including blepharoplasty, there are a number of contraindications, at which presence to the patient is strongly recommended to refuse surgery to lift eyelids.

There is a potential risk of complications (inflammation, allergic reactions) in connection with the use of medications used during anesthesia. Is possible to reduce these risks. There are tests, conduct of which allows to reveal an allergy (or lack thereof) to a pre-known medications.

The patient can independently reduce the risks, if strictly follow the recommendations of your surgeon, without taking into account the opinion of strangers.

Part of postoperative complications are temporary and often directly dependent on the complexity of the cosmetic surgery eye lift and the patient’s individual characteristics. The first few weeks after blepharoplasty in some patients is not excluded difficulty in closing eyelids during sleep, a little more noticeable lacrimation, tension seams. Do not panic. These symptoms disappear soon. Remember that the final result after blepharoplasty is evaluated 2 months after eyelid lifting, no earlier.

After blepharoplasty in San Antonio can occur asymmetry in the eyelids area, dry eyes and an uneven amount of fatty tissue in the operated area. These phenomena are rare, and if necessary, can be additional corrected, usually under local anesthesia. Visual impairment after blepharoplasty are rare.

The complication arises from the accumulation of blood behind the eyeball, which puts pressure on the optic nerve. When removing the excessive skin volume may arise “outcrop sclera”. The lower eyelid is not fully covers the eyeball and the sclera is visible to a greater extent or eversion of the lower eyelid. These cases often put before the fact the need for repetitive corrective surgery on the eyelids.

The first days after blepharoplasty in San Antonio is recommended more rest, and, of course, comply with all recommendations of your doctor.

After 2-3 days you can watch TV or read in the usual volume.

The operated area is not highly recommended to disturb, so the resumption of wearing contact lenses is not recommended earlier than 2 weeks after cosmetic surgery on eyes.

In most cases, the patient returns to normal life through 7-10 days after blepharoplasty.

Healing takes place a little faster or a little slower in different patients, so it is especially necessary for women find out at the surgeon when you can apply makeup on the eyelids.

In most cases, makeup may be used not earlier than 10-14 days after blepharoplasty. The first time after blepharoplasty correctionon the eyelids is recommended to protect the eyes from direct sunlight.

The first month after surgery eyelid is strongly recommended to avoid strenuous exercise. Temporary is a prohibition on visiting the baths and saunas, use of alcoholic beverages.

Preliminary result of the eyelid lifting you can appreciate by about 3-5 days after blepharoplasty. But remember that this is not the final result. For some time after cosmetic blepharoplasty the swelling persists, there may be a tension in sutures. The eyes may look a little oddly, because that tissue has not yet returned to normal.

Eventually, the naturalness of result of the cosmetic surgery on eyes is enhanced, and you can evaluate the effect of blepharoplasty no earlier than 2 months later. There may be a little lacrimation during recovery. In the event you have any questions, please contact us for answers.

It is impossible to get rid of postoperative scars, but rest assured that they will be hardly noticeable that people around you are unlikely will guess about the cosmetic surgery under eyes.
